Where is RJ Barrett from? Early life and family

RJ Barrett was born to parents Rowan Barrett and Kesha Duhaney on June 14, 2000. He was born in Toronto, Canada.

RJ is named after his father, Rowan Barrett. His father is a former basketball player.

His father currently works as Executive Vice President and General Manager of Canada men’s basketball. Moreover, RJ Barret’s mother, Kesha Duhaney, was also a nationally ranked track-and-field athlete at St. John’s.

Hence, RJ Barrett walked in his parent’s footsteps. RJ has a brother, Nathan Barrett. In fact, Nathan also wishes to play for the NBA.

RJ was a basketball enthusiast since childhood. He resided in many countries because of his father’s associations and work.

Furthermore, he spent most of his early childhood overseas. RJ attended a French school while the family resided in France.

His mother, Kesha, taught him English at home. Consequently, RJ is fluent in both English and French.

He liked playing the piano as a child. At the same time, he indulged himself in football, 100 meters sprinting, and high jump.

RJ used to flex his basketball skills in front of his father’s friends and their children.
