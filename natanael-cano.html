Who is Natanael Cano?

Natanael Cano is a Mexican Rapper, Musician, and YouTuber. He is best known for his fusion of trap music and regional Mexican corridors Tumbados. In order to interact with fans and share their experiences, he frequently uploads content to his YouTube account and other online platforms.

Cano is best known for being signed by the Mexican Label Rancho Humilde and winning the Premio Juventud Awards for spicy regional songs.

Additionally, he made a lot of highly successful albums and singles, including Compa Blady, Amor Tumbado, Todo Es Diferente, Musica Pal Barrio, and Mi Nuevo Yo.

Furthermore, Natanael has affiliations with Warner Music Latin and Apple Music. Cano also collaborates with many brands on his YouTube channel. He enjoys creating lifestyle, challenges, and travel content for his huge online fan base.
