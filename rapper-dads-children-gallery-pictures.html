Some of your favorite rappers have the most adorable kids! From Kanye West to Chance the Rapper, we’ve rounded up the sweetest snaps of these music industry titans, and their mini me’s! Yeezy is such a proud dad-of-four. The rapper shares his kiddos North, Saint, Psalm, and Chicago with reality star Kim Kardashian. Here, he’s pictured with Saint before throwing the first pitch at a baseball game in his hometown of Chicago. Chance the Rapper on the other hand, pictured here with his adorable daughter Kensli Bennett, also shares a second daughter Marli Grace Bennett, with wife Kirsten Corley. The rapper opened up to Jimmy Kimmel about the challenges of raising two children. “It’s just way harder, honestly, having two kids than having one. Mathematically, it makes sense, but in my mind, it didn’t register until I actually had two kids,” he said. “People make the diaper part seem so tough. I would much rather do diapers if I didn’t have to wake up in the middle of the night.”
