Jennifer Syme Biography

Jennifer Syme Early Life    

Late Jennifer Syme was born Jennifer Maria Syme on 7th December 1972 in Pico Rivera, California. 

She was the only child to parents Charles Syme (father) and Maria St. John(mother). She was raised in her hometown in Pico Rivera.    

Career As An Actress And Personal Assitant

Jennifer Syme started to work along with director David Lynch, as his assistant. She made her film debut as an actor and production assistant from the 1997 movie .

Later, she worked as a personal assistant to Dave Navarro then to Red Hot Chili Peppers band.

Tragic Death Of Jennifer Syme

Jennifer Syme met with an accident on 2nd April 2001. She was returning from the party of Marilyn Manson and her jeep crashed into a row of parked cars on Cahuenga Boulevard in Lost Angeles. Jennifer died at the spot.
