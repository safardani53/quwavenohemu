Elle Brooke and Sky Bri top Pornhubs UK 'Year In Review' 2023 - but a surprising trend has rocket

BRIT influencer Elle Brooke has been crowned Pornhub's most searched star for 2023.

Surrey born Brooke, 25, fought off long-running global leader Abella Danger, 28, to secure the saucy UK number one spot.

The boxer and OnlyFans star also beat Sky Bri, 24, who finished at number two on the popular porn site's 'Year In Review'.

But overall naughty users have become increasingly attracted to older performers.

Pornhub said mature videos were in their "golden age" for 2023.

Across the world, the mature category exploded in popularity by more than 69 per cent.

It's now the second most popular category among blokes, and the fifth for women.

But in the UK alone mature had an even bigger surge.

Searches for "mature British" have rocketed a whooping 751 per cent.

The mature category is the third most watched, and randy Brits are 55 per cent more likely to watch it compared to other countries.

Data has also revealed more people are interested in sex robots.

"Search trends over the years always coincide with what’s trending in technology," said sexpert Dr Laurie Betito.

"Right now, we are seeing a lot about the development of ‘sex robots,’ so who wouldn’t be curious about that?"

The UK is now Pornhub's fifth most popular country in terms of site visits, moving down from second place last year.

But Brits are now spending longer on the platform, lasting an average of 10 minutes and 31 seconds.

Nerds might be interested to learn that Android owners use Pornhub more than iPhone, making up 51.7 per cent of mobile users.

Google Chrome is the most popular browser by far too.
