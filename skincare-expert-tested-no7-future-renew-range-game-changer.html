I'm a skincare expert and tested No7's new Future Renew range - it was a total game changer

THERE’S a new skincare range on the block and both customers and dermatologists are raving over its magical, scientifically-backed abilities. 

Thanks to celebrities like Hailey Bieber, we’ve all heard of peptides, which are amino acids that make up certain proteins needed by the skin.

Specifically, they can help stimulate your skin to make collagen, which can lead to firmer, younger looking skin. 

When Dr Adel heard how the key ingredients of No7’s Future Renew are two “super peptides” that help the skin’s natural repair process, she jumped at the chance to test it out for herself. 

And now, the dermatologist has taken to her TikTok account to share her honest thoughts on the skincare range. 

“A few months ago, I got a really exciting package containing these top secret skincare products from No7, which contain a newly discovered super peptide blend,” she told viewers as she opened the foiled package up.

Dressed in blue scrubs with her brunette mane down to frame her face, @dermatology.doctor continued: “These were the final samples from the lab, which didn’t even have packaging yet. I felt so special.” 

Squeezing some of the serum onto her cheeks before rubbing the product in, she shared her excitement at being able to be one of the first to try the products out. 

She then sat in front of her laptop, where before and after snaps of a woman using the products could be seen with her fine lines and wrinkles visibly reduced. 

Over this, Dr Adel wrote that the range was tested on roughly 4,200 people over 37 clinical studies.

“When I got home, the final packaged product was finally here. There’s a serum, eye cream, day cream and night cream.

“The new range, driven by this world class peptide technology, which can’t be found in any other product, reverses visible signs of damage by supporting the skin’s natural repair process.” 

“I’ve loved using these products over the last few months and they’re now available for you guys to try so go and check it out,” she added as the clip came to a close. 

Viewers were intrigued by the range and took to the comments to share their thoughts.

While another shared: “So tempted to try the serum!”
