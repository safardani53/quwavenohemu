Beauty of the Day: 5 Secrets of Heidi Klum's Style

Heidi Klum has been a runway mainstay for nearly three decades, having walked in countless designer shows, covered the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit issue and become the first German-born Victoria’s Secret Angel. But that’s not all, the mom of four also has several businesses two fragrances and a successful television career (she’s been nominated for six Emmy Awards!) as a host and judge on Project Runway and America’s Got Talent. Basically, she’s the definition of a #girlboss.

All the while, Heidi has stayed true to her fashion roots as a fearless red carpet style icon and ageless beauty. While she admittedly runs two to three times a week to maintain her fit physique, she’s talked openly about her inside-out approach to beauty (i.e. skipping the carbs in favor of egg whites and smoothies) and keeping her skincare routine low-maintenance.
