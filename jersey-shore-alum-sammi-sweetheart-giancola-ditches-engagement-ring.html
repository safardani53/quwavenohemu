Jersey Shore alum Sammi 'Sweetheart' Giancola ditches engagement ring again after 'split' from fianc

JERSEY Shore alum Sammi "Sweetheart" Giancola was spotted again without her engagement ring.

The former MTV star has reportedly "split" from fiancé Christian Biscardi.

Sources told Us Weekly the ex Jersey Shore star wasn't wearing her ring at yesterday's opening of her new store Sweetheart Coast.

The insider explained: "Sammi was not wearing her engagement ring. She was posing for photos with her left hand in her pocket.”

“She was in a good mood. Had a genuine smile when she spoke to people. It seemed like she was in her element — meeting fans and bringing awareness to her store. She was very gracious to everyone.”

Sammie also told Whitney Ullman in a video for The City Pulse of her new shop: “For those of you know that me, you know I’m a true Jersey Shore girl, and I’ve been coming down to Ocean City since I was a little girl. I’m so excited. I feel like I belong here.”

Last weekend, the once TV star took to TikTok to share a clip of her mouthing along with previous comments made by Kourtney Kardashian on Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

Sammi mouthed along: "My vibe right now is just living life."

The camera angle changed as the Sammi continued: "I am just here to live life."

While filming from a low angle, Sammi quoted Kourtney: "I was just there living life."

For the final quote, Sammi showed off her flowy top and jeans as she said: "You can't say we didn't live life."

Earlier this month, Sammi shared a self-tanning tutorial and was not wearing her engagement ring.

In Touch Weekly also recently reported the couple, who got engaged in March 2019, have unfollowed each other on Instagram.

Christian also appears to have deleted most of his photos with Sammi.

His Facebook relationship status was also set to single, but that's since been cleared.

As for Sammi, she hasn't posted a picture with Christian since January.
