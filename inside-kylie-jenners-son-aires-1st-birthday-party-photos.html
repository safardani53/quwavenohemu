Inside Kylie Jenner's Son Aire's 1st Birthday Party: Photos

Birthday boy! Kylie Jenner celebrated son Aire‘s 1st birthday with a lavish racing-themed party two days after throwing daughter, Stormi, a bash of her own.

“Aire day today,” the Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum, 25, wrote via her Instagram Story on Thursday, February 2.

Jenner showed off the festive décor inside her home that tied in with the sporty theme, including car and checker balloons. There was also a circular ball pit for the kids to jump into that featured a racetrack around the edges.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder’s baby boy was gifted two kids’ cars to drive and an orange motorcycle for when he’s able to ride around on his own.

The Kardashians star, who shares both Stormi, 5, and Aire, 12 months, with ex-boyfriend Travis Scott, made sure the race car vibe spread to the outside as well. She put up a fake road with balloons that read “Route 1” and led to the tunnel that was left over from the Stormi’s World bash.

The “Route 1” decorations paid homage to Aire’s age, as well as the historic roadway that stretches along the coast of California and beyond.

“AIRE. my son, my moon, my stars. best year of my life with you,” Jenner wrote via Instagram alongside a video montage of her youngest child. “You complete us my angel. mommy loves you. happy 1st birthday. may God always bless you.”

Kim Kardashian also paid tribute to Aire on his special day, sharing a picture of the little man with her 5-year-old daughter, Chicago.

“Happy birthday sweet Aire! We love you so much!” the Skims founder, 42, wrote via her Instagram Story on Thursday. “The cousins just adore you and can’t wait to always play with you! We got you for life! We can’t wait for a lifetime of memories.”

Jenner and Scott, 30, initially introduced their fans to their son as Wolf, after his February 2022 arrival. However, the mother of two revealed one month later that the baby’s name had changed.

“FYI our son’s name isn’t Wolf anymore,” the Life of Kylie alum wrote via her Instagram Story at the time. “We just really didn’t feel like it was him. Just wanted to share because I keep seeing Wolf everywhere.”

Less than two weeks before her second child’s birthday, Jenner finally gave fans a glimpse of the little one’s face and revealed his moniker. “I love you Aire Webster,” grandma Kris Jenner commented on the January 21 Instagram post.

Us Weekly broke the news last month that Kylie and the “Sicko Mode” rapper called it quits. The pair have been dating on and off since 2017.
