Sydney Sweeney ENGAGED to Jonathan Davino as Euphoria star flaunts '$150K' ring after keeping romanc

EUPHORIA star Sydney Sweeny is 'engaged' to longtime boyfriend Jonathan Davino after keeping her romance under wraps for years.

The actress is know for her roles on the HBO series, as well as the hit shows The Handmaid's Tale and The White Lotus.

A source close to the rising star told E! that "she and Johnny refer to each other as fiancés."

"They're very cute and happy together."

Engagement rumors began swirling after Sydney, 24, was spotted wearing a large diamond ring on her left ring finger, according to Page Six.

The actress was spotted spending time with a friend while out and about in Encino, California earlier this week.

She smiled as she chatted, enjoying a sunny day on the west coast.

Sydney sported a pink sweatshirt, black leggings and Uggs. She topped it off with sunglasses.

Gemologist and founder of The Clear Cut, Olivia Landau, estimated that the star's ring is "a 3.5-4 carat round brilliant diamond."

She noted: "Something similar could range from $50-$150K, depending on quality."

Sydney and Jonathan have been dating for many years.

ROAD TO ROMANCE

According to Elle, the duo were first linked in 2018.

The couple is notoriously private, however.

They have been spotted showing PDA only a handful of times.

Just Jared reported that the couple was seen celebrating Jonathan's birthday together at Tao Chicago in 2019.

A year later, they enjoyed a romantic trip to Hawaii.

Most recently, Sydney and her beau held hands as they enjoyed a trip to a West Hollywood flea market.

Jonathan hails from Chicago and works as a restauranteur.

His family owns the well-known Italian restaurant Pompei.

WHY SO PRIVATE?

Sydney opened up about her decision to keep her love life private in a February 2022 interview with Cosmopolitan.

She told the outlet she doesn't "date people in the spotlight."

Sydney went on: "I don't date actors or musicians or anyone in entertainment because I can just be normal Syd that way and it's easiest."

She went on: "I have a great support system. I have people who will battle for me and allow me to be on the pedestal and shine without making me feel like, 'Oh no, I'm shining too bright and I need to step back.'

"I look for a best friend. I need to be able to be with someone who I can literally hang out with 24/7 and never get sick of and we laugh every single day."

ALL ABOUT SYDNEY

Sydney is a somewhat new, but well-known actress who hails from Washington.

She auditioned for her first role at age 12 after learning that an indie production was coming to town.

Speaking with Coveteur, she said: "They were holding auditions, so I begged my parents to let me audition by putting together a five-year business plan presentation."

After she landed her first acting role, Sydney and her family moved to Los Angeles where she could focus on making a name for herself.
