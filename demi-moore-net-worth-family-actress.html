What is Demi Moore's net worth?

DEMI Moore is one of the most famous actresses to date.

The Hollywood star also stands for women’s equality within the industry.

Who is Demi Moore?

Demi Moore was born on November 11, 1962, in Roswell, New Mexico.

She was born with the name Demetria Gene Guynes but adopted the last name of her first husband, Freddy Moore.

The American actress and film producer has been nominated for a Critic’s Choice Movie Award, two Golden Globe Awards, a Primetime Emmy Award, and a Screen Actors Guild Award. 

Despite being one of the highest-paid actresses through the 1980s and 1990s, she has fought for equal salaries for women in Hollywood.

Moore is also known for her looks and media image, recognized as a sex symbol. 

In 2019, her memoir titled Inside Out became a New York Times Best Seller.

Who are Demi Moore's parents?

Moore was born to her parents Charles Harmon and Virginia King.

Moore had a better relationship with her stepfather, Dan Guynes, who died by suicide in 1980.

After a battle with cancer, Moore's biological father died in 1997 and Moore's mother had a long arrest record which included DUIs and arson.

As a child, Moore suffered from Strabismus, a condition in which the eyes do not align when looking at an object, but had two operations to correct it.

Moore also suffered from kidney dysfunction.

Is Demi Moore married?

Moore has been married to musician Freddy Moore (1981-1985), actor Bruce Willis (1987-2000), and actor Ashton Kutcher (2005-2013).

She has three children with Willis, Rumer, Scout, and Tallulah. 

Rumer, the eldest daughter, is an actress and singer who has made many film appearances including Hostage (2005), The House Bunny (2008), Sorority Row (2009), and Once Upon a Time in Hollywood (2019).  

She was also the winner of ABC’s Dancing with the Stars in season 20.
