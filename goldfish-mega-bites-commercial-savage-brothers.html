Who are the actors in the Goldfish Mega Bites commercial?

In late February of 2022, Goldfish - crackers manufactured by Pepperidge Farm - dropped a new commercial featuring two 1990s icons: the Savage brothers, Fred and Ben.

The two discuss the current era of bringing back old movies and television shows into a modern context.

Who is Fred Savage?

Born on July 9, 1976, Fred Savage is an actor and director mostly known for his role as Kevin Arnold in The Wonder Years.

Throughout his lengthy career, Fred has earned a multitude of awards and nominations - including a People's Choice Award.

Other television shows Fred has appeared in include The Twilight Zone, Morningstar/Eveningstar, The Outer Limits, Boy Meets World, Even Stevens, That's So Raven, Drake & Josh, Unfabulous, Phil of the Future, and Hannah Montana.

Who is Ben Savage?

Born on September 13, 1980, Ben Savage is an actor mostly known for playing the lead role of Cory Matthews on Boy Meets World and its reboot, Girl Meets World.
