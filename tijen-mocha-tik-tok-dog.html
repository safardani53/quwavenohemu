Who is TikTok star Tijen Mocha and who is her dog Mocha Pom?

IF there's one thing guaranteed to please social media users it's an adorable dog and Mocha the Pomeranian, also known as Mocha Pom, certainly fits the bill.

Mocha and her owner, Tijen, are hugely successful TikTok, Instagram and YouTube influencers and have turned their online presence into a fully-fledged business - complete with merchandise.

Who are Tijen Mocha and Mocha Pom?

Tijen Mocha and Mocha Pom are the online accounts for an Australian woman named Tijen and her dog Mocha.

Mocha is a black and tan Pomeranian who rose to fame after having numerous Instagram and TikTok videos that went viral.

Mocha and her owner Tijen love to go on adventures together where Tijen takes photos and videos to share with her followers.

The pair have garnered a huge social media following since launching their careers as influencers.

What do Tijen Mocha and Mocha Pom do on Instagram and TikTok?

Tijen and Mocha share a TikTok account, but on Instagram there is a separate account for Tijen (@tijenmocha) as well as the official Mocha Pom account (@mochapom_).

On TikTok, Tijen posts hilarious videos and skits of what her and Mocha have been getting up to.

They are also shared to Mocha's Instagram account, where more still pictures of the cute pup are also posted.

Similarly, Tijen uploads adorable photos to her own Instagram account, as well as the funny videos that catapulted her and her dog to fame.

On TikTok, Tijen and Mocha have over 14million followers, while Mocha has 408k fans on Instagram and Tijen has nearly 1000.

Tijen and Mocha also have an official YouTube account where they have over 1.6million subscribers.

What is Tijen Mocha and Mocha Pom's net worth?

Online reports of Tijen and Mocha's net worth vary greatly.

Some reports suggest the pooch and owner duo have made $5million from their social media stardom, while others believe them to be worth just over $200,000.

The pair are likely to have made their cash through brand deals on social media, as well as their special Mocha merchandise that they sell through their website.

The range includes hoodies and t-shirts, ranging from 55 AUD to 98 AUD.

What did Tijen Mocha do before becoming an influencer?

Not much is known about Tijen's career before finding success online.

She has previously revealed why she decided to start an Instagram page for her dog in the first place.

Tijen told HelloBark.com: "I started to take a lot of photos of Mocha and I wanted to share these with others to make them happy as much as she makes me.

"I spend about one to two hours daily on Instagram. Over the time [Mocha] has had an Instagram page, we have made some amazing friends all over the world. I like to look at their posts and engage with people that comment on Mocha's photos too."

What is Tijen Mocha's real name?

Tijen's real name is Tijen Simseker.
