Jill Duggar breaks another major family rule with big decision about newborn son Freddy's birth

JILL Duggar has broken another major family rule with a big decision about her newborn son's birth.

The Counting On alum, 31, said she had a planned C-section "a little early" and welcomed her son, Frederick, on July 7.

Jill and Derick decided to have the baby via c-section, instead of at home, which is one of the stringent rules set in the Duggar household.

The couple announced the birth of their son on the family blog.

Her previous births to their two sons: Israel, six, and Samuel, four were also done in a hospital, after the former reality star suffered complications.
