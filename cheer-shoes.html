Amazon's Most Reviewed Cheer Shoes

Get ready to cheer with the most reviewed cheer shoes on Amazon.

Cheer shoes are an essential asset of cheerleaders. Wearing cheer shoes allows you to perform athletic movement gracefully and comfortably.

Good cheer shoes should support your ankle and the bottom of your foot while jumping, tumbling, and doing any stunts.

And another important feature of cheer shoes is that they are light-weight. When you are light on your feet, you can smoothly perform dance movements and difficult stunts.

What Are The Benefits Of Cheer Shoes?

If you want to seriously get involved in cheerleading, then you surely want to have cheer shoes for practice.

But that so doesn’t mean you can ignore training. You need to practice aggressively to get perfect at cheerleading.

 Some cheerleaders wear sneakers for training which don’t support you with cheerleading. There are number of benefits of choosing cheer shoes over sneakers.

Incredibly light

Sneakers you get in the market are bulky. They are not good for athletic movement. Though you may find some light-weight sneakers as well, but they will surely not help you with cheerleading.

Cheer shoes are light in weight, allowing you to jump high and tumble effortlessly.  When you feel light on your feet, you will be able to do a stunt on the ground and air with ease.

Easy clean

Being tidy is important for cheerleaders. You need to look neat from head to toe while performing. So, there is no way to compromise on your shoe.

Most of the cheer shoes are white. So, for this reason, cheerleaders tend to wear white sneakers over cheer shoes.

Easy clean is another reason to switch to cheer shoes because sneakers are not easy to clean.

Decent padding

Sneakers don’t have decent padding. The shoe you wear while cheerleading needs to have an excellent and strategic padding system to provide enough support.

Decently padded cheer shoes provide cushioning to protect against impact. While doing a stunt on the ground, you will need a great footbed to protect against serious impact.

Rubber outsole

Flexibility is a must while cheerleading. Your shoe needs to support all your moves just the way you want without making you uncomfortable. Sneakers will not help you with movement.

As cheer shoes are specially designed for cheerleaders, these shoes help you move in the direction you want to. The rubber outsole provides just the right flex you need for cheerleading.

Lace locking system

Of Couse, you don’t want your shoe to slip off your foot while performing. Cheer shoes come with a lace locking system to avoid any kind of slip.

With cheerleading shoes, you can jump as much as you want without worrying about your shoes.

However, if you choose sneakers, you will not be able to give full while cheerleading because they don’t have a good lace locking system.

Cheer shoes ensure you stay on the ground all the time during the performance.

Finger Grooves

Cheer shoes come with finger grooves design to support cheerleaders. Finger grooves become important when doing air stunts.

Finger grooves provide stability to the flyer while performing a stunt. Cheer shoe with finger grooves doesn’t just give stability to a flyer but make the job of bases easier.

Comfort, Fit And Style

Besides the mentioned features, you also need to look for the comfort, fit and style of the cheer shoes.

The cheer shoes you choose must support you to jump, kick and perform stunts. Whether you perform on the ground or in the air, cheer shoes must support your foot movement.

Cheerleaders spend an entire day in the shoes jumping and doing stunts. It is extremely necessary to have the right shoe for practice.

The comfort that shoes provide can make much difference in your performance. When the shoe is not comfortable enough, you won’t be able to do all stunts as you want to.

Cheer shoes must feel snug on your feet, allowing you to perform and move naturally.

However, these shoes also need to have just the right space for your toe to move freely. Also, the shoe you choose for cheerleading should be tight around your heel.

The comfort of Cheer shoes is defined by how well they support you to jump, tumble and do stunts.

One of the important criteria to buy cheer shoes is to check how well they fit on your shoe. Even if you buy expensive cheer shoe, there is no meaning of buying expensive shoes if it doesn’t fit right.

You will obviously find many cheer shoe brands in the market. And all cheer shoe brand has different sizing.

The size you fit in one brand may not be right for another brand. You may have to go one size up or one size down, depending upon the brand.

Do check the brand website to know your right size. Cheer shoes that fit you perfectly help improve your performance and also help in avoiding injuries.

Most cheerleading shoe comes in white with strips or attractive designs. However, the cheerleading team can have specific design requirements.

If you want to join the team, get proper information about their cheer shoe style.

If you have not decided on the team, then you can choose any style you like. You will find varieties of cheer shoe designs, but mostly in white. You can select a cheer shoe according to your choice.

Don’t use cheer shoes on a rough surface, and don’t wear them for a tough task. You may wear cheer shoes for jogging, running but not for the gym.

To increase the life of cheer shoes, better to use them only for cheerleading.

Another essential thing you need to know about cheer shoes is how to wear them right.

The lacing system is an important feature of cheer shoes. Cheer shoes have a unique lacing system to secure your feet and to fix your feet in place.

Cheerleaders tend to pull the lace to tighten the shoes. The lacing system in cheer shoes is designed in a way to secure your feet and keep them out of your way throughout your performance.

If you pull the lace tightly, it may break. Be careful while pulling the lace. However, pulling laces tight is terrible for your feet. Cheer shoe comfort comes from buying the right size, not pulling the laces.
