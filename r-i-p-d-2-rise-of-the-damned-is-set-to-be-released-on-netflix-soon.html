R.I.P.D. 2: Rise of the Damned Is Set To Be Released On Netflix Soon

R.I.P.D. 2: Rise of the Damned, the brand-new sequel to 2013’s supernatural action-comedy film R.I.P.D.: Rest in Peace Department, will premiere exclusively on the popular streaming platform Netflix on Tuesday, November 15, 2022, at midnight Pacific Time (PT). However, the film will only be distributed in the United States.

The film’s sequel was directed by Paul Leyden. Along with Andrew Klein, Leyden wrote the script. R.I.P.D. 2: Rise of the Damned’s cinematographer is Bruno Degrave, while J. Peter Robinson composed the music. Since the release of the film’s official trailer, fans have been eager to watch how events develop in the thrilling sequel, R.I.P.D. 2: Rise of the Damned.

R.I.P.D. 2: Rise of the Damned will be available on Netflix.

R.I.P.D. 2: Rise of the Damned, an action-comedy film, will be released exclusively on Netflix on Tuesday, November 15, 2022. The film is set in the American West in 1876. The story will center on Sheriff Roy Pulsipher, who is entrusted with rescuing the whole planet when a door to hell opens up in Red Creek, an ancient mining town.

According to the IMDb summary for the brand-new film:

“‘R.I.P.D.2: Rise of the Damed,’ set in the American West in 1876, is a spiritual (excuse the pun) sequel to 2013’s ‘R.I.P.D.’ Sheriff Roy Pulsipher isn’t happy about dying in a shootout with a legendary outlaw gang, but he does have a second opportunity to return to Earth after being recruited by the R.I.P.D. (Rest In Peace Department).”

According to the IMDb synopsis:

“However, avenging his own death may have to take a second place to rescue the world as a portal to hell opens in the abandoned mining town of Red Creek, endangering not just the residents… but all of mankind.”

Sheriff Roy Pulsipher will have a lot on his plate as he attempts to save the human race. It is fair to assume that the audience is in for an exciting and captivating ride.

How is the official trailer for the upcoming Netflix sequel film?

The official trailer for the film provides fans with tantalizing peeks at what to anticipate from the sequel to R.I.P.D.: Rest in Peace Department. Sheriff Roy Pulsipher is shown coming to the Rest in Peace Department in the official trailer, where Jeanne greets him. She informs him that their mission is to return renegade souls who have avoided judgment to hell. Following that, the teaser promises a series of exhilarating adventures and deadly scenarios as the two starts on a journey to rescue mankind.

Who are the next film’s cast members?

Jeffrey Donovan plays Roy Pulsipher, Penelope Mitchell plays Jeanne, Rachel Adedeji plays Avatar Roy, Evlyne Oyedokun plays Avatar Jeanne, Richard Brake plays Otis Clairborne, Jake Choi plays Slim Samuels, Stephanie Levi-John plays Beverley, and Tilly Keeper plays Charlotte Pulsipher, among others.
