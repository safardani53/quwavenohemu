Nate shared his 15-minute recipe using the countertop gadget - and people can’t believe how easy it is to make. 

The foodie, known online as @boredoflunch, regularly shares quick and easy recipes with his 623,000 followers. 

In a new TikTok reel, he said: “I am obsessed with this air fryer prawn toast. It’s 38 calories a triangle. 

“Simple mash up 150 grams of prawns with some sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. 

“Spread across three to four slices of bread, and then dunk that into some sesame seeds.
