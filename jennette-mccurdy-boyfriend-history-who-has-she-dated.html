Jennette McCurdy boyfriend history: Who has she dated?

NICKELODEON star Jennette McCurdy has made no secret about her past relationships.

The iCarly alum, who left Hollywood behind after her mom died from cancer in 2013, has dated mainly fellow actors.

Is Jennette McCurdy dating?

As of August 2022, Jennette is not dating anyone as far as is known.

The Sam & Cat actress, who was born on June 26, 1992, in Los Angeles, has never been married.

Who has Jennette McCurdy dated?

The child star has been linked with several men - and some of the relationships have been public.

Since 2016, the actress, who values her privacy, has been quiet about who she may or may not be dating. Here's what we know:

Jesse Carere

Jennette dated fellow actor Jesse Carere for a year after they both starred in the sci-fi series Between, which aired on Netflix in 2015 and 2016.

At the time, Jennette told Vulture: “This is maybe staking a big claim in my future self, but I would say that I certainly love him right, you know?”

Max Ehrich

Before her time on the show with Jesse, Jennette was rumoured to have struck a relationship with Demi Lovato's ex-fiancé, Max Ehrich in 2010.

Actor Max starred in an episode of iCarly that year.

Fans speculated Jennette and Max were a couple, but the blond actress confirmed they were never romantically engaged.

In 2016, Max set the record straight, tweeting Jennette "was always just a friend".

Andre Drummond

In August 2013, Jennette had a brief fling with NBA star Andre Drummond, with whom she struck up a friendship on social media.

The short-lived relationship ended the following year.

Paul Glaser

Prior to this, Jennette dated Paul Glaser, while she was starring on the hit Nickelodeon show.

Paul worked behind the scenes as a script manager on the iCarly set.

The actress told Vulture about her relationship in 2015: “I put my feelings of love toward someone other than my mother, who had been the receiver of all that love up until that point."

The pair broke up before Jennette's mum passed away in 2013.

Jennette told Vulture: “I needed to go through that loss on my own.

“I felt that I would put too much of myself into another person and become very attached.”

Graham Patrick Martin

The podcast host was also linked to Two And A Half Men Graham Patrick Martin as a teen, although they never confirmed they were an item.
