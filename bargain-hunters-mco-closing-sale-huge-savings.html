Bargain hunters go wild for M&amp;Co's closing sale with HUGE savings including 140 dresses slashed to

BARGAIN hunters will know that when a high street store announces they’re closing their branches for good, it can lead to huge reductions. 

So when news of M&Co shutting 170 stores nationwide emerged, fans of the high street chain started to form an orderly queue to check out what they could get their hands on as they mourned one of their favourite stores. 

And it wasn’t long before shoppers were telling each other about their amazing purchases and how much money they’d managed to save in the process. 

One excited bargain hunter recently took to Facebook group Extreme Couponing And Bargains UK to share her – and how she’d saved herself a massive £130. 

Attaching three images of a stylish burgundy real leather dress which also comes in black, she wrote: “Sad that M&Co is closing but for bargain hunters, it’s a delight.

“Got this Sonder real leather dress, £140 for £10.”
