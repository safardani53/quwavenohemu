10 Best gymnastic mats 2022: from crash mats to wedge mats

GYMNASTICS is one of the toughest sports out there, combining flexibility, strength and courage so practice is crucial whether you're a seasoned pro or a newbie.

A fundamental part of gymnastics is tumbling, whether that's on the floor, beam or while vaulting and the best way to get your somersaults, backward walkovers and handsprings up to tip-top condition is by practising on a gymnastics mat and the best part is, there are lots of gymnastic mats you can get for your own home.

From wedges for skills that need momentum, like forward rolls and somersaults, to longer mats for sequences of tumbles there's something out there to suit whatever you or your little one needs.

If you're worried about not having enough space at home, then opt for a mat that you can pack away, and most designs are made from material that you can easily wipe down or even use in the pool if they're inflatable.
