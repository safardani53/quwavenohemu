Who is Jillian Michaels?

Jillian Michaels is an American personal trainer and fitness expert, television personality ,and businesswoman.

Michaels is the mother of two children - a daughter, Lukensia, adopted from Haiti ,and a son, Phoenix, whom her former partner, Heidi Rhoades, gave birth to.

Even with her busy schedule, Michaels told People that, "I want to be really present and enjoy all the nuances of their growth and development. I don’t want to rush any of it.”

She is an Emmy-nominated television talent and was a well-known trainer on the American TV series, The Biggest Loser, where she helped contestants lose weight and reach their fitness goals.

Michaels is a New York Times bestselling author of books like, Winning By Losing.

She also created an app, which has won Apple and Google Play awards. It is called, The Fitness App, and provides customized workouts and meal plans that can be streamed anywhere.

She has made appearances on E! News, Access, Insider, etc., and has been featured on the covers of magazines like Woman's Health and Healthline.

What is her net worth?

As of July 2022, Jillian Michaels' net worth is approximately $18million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

After struggling with her weight as a child, she decided to make it her dream to focus on fitness and help others around her achieve their own health goals.

In 2012, she launched a 90-day weight loss program called, Body Revolution and a few years later created an at-home workout called, BODYSHRED.

Her structured routines and programs have made her a world-renowned health and fitness expert.

Is Jillian Michaels married?

On November 27, 2021, Michaels announced on Instagram that she was engaged to DeShanna Marie Minuto, who is a New York City fashion designer of the brand, Letterino.

Subsequently, PEOPLE announced that Michaels and DeShanna got married on July 11, 2022, at a Miami courthouse.
