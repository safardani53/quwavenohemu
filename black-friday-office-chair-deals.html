    How to clean office chairs

    If you've been working from home for a while, don't forget to clean your office equipment.

    For a mesh office chair, you can simply wipe it down with water and a mild soap or a stain remover on tougher stains.

    You may also want to use a vacuum cleaner to get rid of any loose dirt.

    Products such as Detoll all-in-one and UltraGrime Clothwipes can help you clean your office chair.

    How to protect carpet from your office chair

    If you've set up a home office and your carpet is starting to suffer, you can get a chair mat to protect your carpet from your office chair wheels.

    Chair mats or floor protection mats can stop marks and dents in your carpet.
