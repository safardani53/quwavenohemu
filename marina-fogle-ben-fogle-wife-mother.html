Who is Ben Fogles wife Marina Hunt? The Sun

BEN Fogle is known for his adventures which take him across the globe.

But when he's not hiking up Mount Everest, or trekking to the North Pole, his greatest pleasure lies in being at home with his wife, Marina Fogle.

Who is Ben Fogle's wife Marina?

Marina Fogle is the co-founder of The Bump Class, which prepares mums-to-be for their labour journey, with her sister Dr Chiara Hunt.

The Austrian also has a podcast with her sister called The Parent Hood.

The business savvy sisters are the authors behind The Bump Class: An Expert Guide to Pregnancy, Birth and Beyond, which was released in 2016.

Marina and Ben moved out of their West London home into a period cottage in leafy Henley-on-Thames with their young children.

When did Ben Fogle and Marina Hunt start dating?

Ben met Marina for the first time while they walked their dogs together in Hyde Park and the rest is history.

Since meeting, the couple have enjoyed a private marriage and keep their personal matters out of the spotlight.

However, as The Daily Mail previously reported, Marina has shared how she and Ben often bicker over the dishwasher, which he doesn't load very well.

She said: "There will be pans with plates on top, facing the wrong way. And he even drops knives in point-up, risking serious bodily harm to the poor person who unloads them.

How many children do they have?

Marina and Ben share two children: Ludovic Herbert Richard, born in 2009 and Iona, born in 2011.

Sadly, in 2014 their son Willem was stillborn after an acute placental abruption meant that he was starved of oxygen.
