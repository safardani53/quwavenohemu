Man dies after plunging from top floor of Reading multi-storey car park in front of horrified Christ

A MAN has died after plunging from the top floor of a multi-storey car park in front of horrified Christmas shoppers.

The tragic death happened at the Oracle shopping centre car park in Reading, Berkshire.

A police cordon was put up and several roads were shut as paramedics and police rushed to the scene just after 10am this morning.

Security staff ushered Christmas shoppers away from the scene.

A spokesman for the Oracle said: "We can confirm that an incident occurred this morning at the centre's Riverside car park and sadly the individual has passed away.

"Emergency services were called immediately and we will continue to provide our full support to the authorities throughout their investigations. Our thoughts are with their friends and family at this very difficult time."

A spokesman for Thames Valley Police said: "We are investigating an unexplained but non-suspicious death of a man in Bridge Street, Reading.

"We were called to the scene at 10.09am today.

"Police are currently at the scene and a cordon is in place."

A spokesman for South Central Ambulance Service said: "We received a call at 10.04am and sent two ambulance crews.

"Sadly, one patient was confirmed deceased at the scene."

The victim has not yet been named by police.
