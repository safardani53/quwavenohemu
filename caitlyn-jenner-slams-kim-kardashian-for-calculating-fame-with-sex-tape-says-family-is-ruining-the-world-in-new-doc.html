Caitlyn Jenner slams Kim Kardashian for 'calculating fame' with sex tape &amp; says family is 'ruining t

CAITLYN Jenner has taken aim at Kim Kardashian and the rest of the famous family in a scathing new documentary series.

The controversial former Olympian is set to appear in House of Kardashian, in which she and others will expose Kardashian secrets.

Caitlyn, 73, appeared in a trailer for the documentary that was released on YouTube.

In it, she can be heard saying of her family: "You're kind of in one of two camps: I wanna talk about them or they've ruined the world."

She then closed her eyes as she said: "Oh my god. Anyway, here we go," seemingly bracing for what's to come.

Elsewhere in the trailer, the former Keeping Up With the Kardashians star revealed: "Kimberly calculated from the very beginning: 'How do I become famous?'"

Caitlyn was also asked at one point: "What was it like working with your wife?" referring to Kris Jenner.

She did not respond, instead just laughing.

The Kardashian family has not yet responded to the documentary.

GOING TO WAR

The documentary's impending release was announced in August.

The controversial series from Sky will examine whether the Kardashians are an inspiration to millions of fans or your average reality stars.

An insider told the U.S. Sun: "Millions of fans see the Kardashians as people to aspire to be like, but this new program will unpack whether these women are just selling a superficial fairytale.

"Caitlyn could rock the boat with the Kardashians by agreeing to sign up to the series just with the suggestion that the family is full of untalented and morally bankrupt reality stars.

"She's due to give an exclusive interview and offer up her insight from an insider's perspective, alongside never before seen footage of the Kardashians."

Caitlyn's docuseries interview isn't the first time she's risked the wrath of the Kardashian family.

She appeared on I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here and name-dropped her daughters Kylie and Kendall Jenner, along with their older siblings Kim, Kourtney, and Khloe Kardashian on the show.

She did not, however, talk about Kris.

SAYING TOO MUCH

Caitlyn also caused drama with the family in 2017 with the release of her tell-all memoir, The Secrets of My Life.

In the book she talked about her relationship with Khloe, claiming that it was fractured.

She said that the youngest Kardashian daughter was struggling with Caitlyn's coming out as transgender.

"Khloe has the hardest time with it," she wrote at the time.

"It is something Khloe and I should talk about privately, as we have on many occasions on other sensitive subjects. But we have not, although I have tried. We have not been the same since."

She also claimed that Kris managed and controlled her finances during their marriage.

The Kardashian family reacted to Caitlyn talking about them in her book on their reality TV shows.

At the time, Kris blasted her ex as "very mean-spirited" and dubbed the memoir "inaccurate."
