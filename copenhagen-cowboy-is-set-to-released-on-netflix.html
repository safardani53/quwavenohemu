Copenhagen Cowboy Is Set To Released On Netflix

Copenhagen Cowboy, a new Netflix series from Danish director Nicolas Winding Refn, is set to launch on Thursday, January 5, 2023, at 3 a.m. ET. The plot revolves around a young lady who is hellbent on exacting vengeance on people who have damaged her life. Angela Bundalovic stars as the series’ protagonist, with several others playing important supporting parts. Winding Refn’s return to Danish-language films after his success with Pusher 3 in 2005.

Copenhagen Cowboy is now available on Netflix, and the trailer promises an epic vengeance story.

On November 23, 2022, Netflix released the official trailer for Copenhagen Cowboy, which provides a glimpse inside Nicolas Winding Refn’s beautifully constructed universe, along with various intriguing and interesting characters. The trailer displays some of the show’s action moments briefly. However, arguably the most remarkable part of the teaser is that it concentrates only on defining the tone and aesthetic of the series in a traditional Refn way, rather than disclosing the core idea of Copenhagen Cowboy. According to Metacritic, here’s a synopsis of the series:

“In this six-part series by Nicolas Winding Refn, Miu (Angela Bundalovic), a young lady with magical abilities, goes to Copenhagen to learn about her history and seek justice.”

According to the teaser and description, fans can expect a gripping and elegant vengeance thriller in the vein of Refn’s Pusher flicks and Drive. It has a unique flavor to it and promises to record a fascinating voyage while delving into themes of vengeance, bloodshed, and much more.

Nicholas Winding Refn is a highly regarded Danish director known for films such as Valhalla Rising, the Pusher trilogy, Drive, and The Neon Demon. His films are notable for their distinct aesthetic aspects and compelling characters. Alejandro Jodorowsky, John Cassavetes, and many more are among his major inspirations.

A short glance at the Copenhagen Cowboy cast and additional information

As Miu, the brilliant Angela Bundalovic shines in Copenhagen Cowboy. Bundalovic looks fantastic in the show’s teaser, effortlessly painting the many sides of her character. Fans should anticipate a tremendous performance from her on the program. Angela Bundalovic has been in a number of popular and highly praised programs and films in recent years, including The Rain, Limboland, and Kbenhavn findes ikke, in addition to Winding Refn’s forthcoming series.

The part of Miroslav is played by highly renowned actor Zlatko Buri, who co-stars with Bundalovic. According to numerous rumors, Buri will portray a nasty role in the film, and fans should anticipate a spectacular performance from the legendary actor.

Zlatko Buri has previously appeared in films by Nicolas Winding Refn, including the Pusher trilogy and Bleeder. His other acting credits include to mention a few, 2012, St. George’s Day, and Quit Staring at My Plate. Many additional performers play supporting parts in the series, including Lola Corfixen as Rakel, Fleur Frilund as Jessica, Andreas Lykke Jrgensen as Nicklas, and many more. On Thursday, January 5, 2023, at 3 a.m. ET, Netflix will release the new action thriller Copenhagen Cowboy.
