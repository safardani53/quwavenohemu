Sierra Capri Exclusive Portraits Of The On My Block Star Hollywood Life

‘On My Block’ star Sierra Capri stops by HollywoodLife’s New York City studio. She plays Monse Finnie on the hit Netflix series. The show recently debuted its second season. ‘On My Block,’ co-created Lauren Iungerich, Eddie Gonzalez, and Jeremy Haft, is a coming of age comedy about four bright and street-savvy friends navigating their way through the triumph, pain and the newness of high-school set in the rough inner city. Sierra stars alongside Jason Genao, Diego Tinoco, Brett Gray, Jessica Marie Garcia, and more. At the end of season 2, Monse tells his friends that she’s decided to leave Freeridge but they don’t believe her. She walks away and Ruby, Cesar, and Jamal are kidnapped. The final moments of the season 2 finale feature Monse getting kidnapped as well. ‘On My Block’ left fans with quite the cliffhanger and now they’re waiting with bated breath for a season 3 renewal.
