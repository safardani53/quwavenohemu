Heather Graham Hollywood Life

Bio:

Heather Graham born

Heather Joan Graham (born Heather Joan Graham on January 29, 1970) is an American actress who landed her first major role in the 1989 film, Drugstore Cowboy. Since then she’s starred in Swingers, Shout, Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me, Boogie Nights and she also starred in the TV series Twin Peaks. Heather grew up in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and her dad is a former FBI agent. She was raised Catholic and moved to California when she was 9. She attended UCLA and then dropped out to pursue acting.

Best Known For:

Heather is best known for being an actress and staring in movies like Austin Powers and Boogie Nights.

Personal Life:

Heather has never been married or had kids. She is also estranged from her parents. She has been in several high-profile relationships with actors like: Russell Crowe, Josh Lucas and Matt Dillon. She has famously said she “dodged a bullet” by not marrying and having kids. Saying she’d rather be “happily single” than “unhappily married.”
