Jhonni Blaze Facts: 5 Things To Know About The ‘Love & Hip Hop’ Star

From her promising musical career to her rocky past, the Love & Hip Hop New York newcomer has made her name quite well-known throughout season 5.

1.) She Is A Model & ‘Twerk Queen’

Jhonni, who is reportedly half German, has graced several magazine covers and starred in several music videos for artists such as Chris Brown, French Montana, Young Berg, Jim Jones and Jadakiss. As a former exotic dancer in Houston, Jhonni has also developed a reputation as the ‘Twerk Queen’! Watch out, Miley Cyrus!

2.) She Has Beef With Drake

The LHHNY star once famously claimed that she got intimate with rapper, Drake. As we previously reported, Jhonni claimed that she had a one-night stand with the rapper, but the dancer didn’t stop there according to MediaTakeOut! She also claimed afterwards that he allegedly “threatened her life” because he was afraid that she would go public with their fling. Plus, Jhonni reportedly got jealous when Drake allegedly spent time with another stripper, Lira Galore.

3.) She Has A Sex Tape

Didn’t think Jhonni could get her more controversial? As seen on season 5 of LHHNY, the 24-year-old has to deal with the fall out of her sex tape while she pursues a singing career. Time will only tell if her rocky past will prevent her from being a success in the Hip Hop industry?

4.) She Is A Musician

Jhonni can reportedly play the piano, acoustic, bass guitar, violin, clarinet and drums according to her bio. When she was 17, Jhonni dreamed of attending the prestigious Julliard Performing Arts School. However, with a new single, “Revolver“, coming out on Love & Hip Hop, it looks like the New York native is getting her second chance on VH1. She is also signed to Dollaz Unlimited.

5.) She Has A Temper

Although Jhonni’s real last name is Jzapal, the reality star was nicknamed “Blaze” by Method Man and Redman.“They gave me Blaze, because they said I was always a hothead,”revealed Joni in her bio. Ladies of LHHNY, be warned!
