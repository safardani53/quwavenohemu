Photos From Young Actress To Icon Hollywood Life

Sharon Stone, born on Mar. 10, 1958, is a celebrated Hollywood actress, widely recognized for her performances in iconic movies like Basic Instinct, Casino, and Total Recall. Sharon first got noticed in the 1990 action film Total Recall, but her breakout role as a femme fatale in 1992’s Basic Instinct. From there, she starred in 1995’s Casino. The film won her a Golden Globe, and she even was in the running for an Oscar that year.

Sharon’s other notable 90s films include The Mighty (1998) and The Muse (1999). In the 2000s, she’d appear in Catwoman (2004), Broken Flowers (2005), Alpha Dog (2006), Bobby (2006), Lovelace (2013), The Disaster Artist (2017), and The Laundromat (2019).

Besides her impressive career in movies, Sharon has also graced television. She earned a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Guest Actress in a Drama Series for The Practice. She was in Steven Soderbergh’s Mosaic in 2017 and in 2020, she appeared in the Ryan Murphy series Ratched.
