Why is 'Ellen Degeneres dead' trending?

ELLEN fans were thrown into a frenzy when the star's "death" was trending on social media.

The Ellen DeGeneres show host was a victim of a hoax which left the world believing she had died - but she is alive and well.

Why is 'Ellen Degeneres dead' trending?

The world-famous comedian and TV host was the target of false claims on social media saying she had passed away.

User-generated posts announced the 'fake news' on X, formerly known as Twitter.

Hashtag RIPEllen took fans by a storm and sent them in full panic mode.

But the trending posts were part of a phenomenon of fake rumors about Ellen.
