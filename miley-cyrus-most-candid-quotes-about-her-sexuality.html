Wasn’t It Obvious?

Miley cited a throwback photo of herself, Taylor Swift, Demi Lovato and Hannah Montana costar Emily Osment as proof that fans should’ve known she was bisexual long before she came out.

In an August 2023 TikTok video, she displayed the squad photo which shows her rocking a T-shirt and sweatpants while the other three stars are dressed to the nines.

“This picture has become a meme where it says, ‘Be the Miley of your friend group,’” Miley explained in the clip. “If you guys didn’t know I was bisexual from this damn picture, I don’t know what’s wrong with you. … I mean hello! Look at ‘em.”
