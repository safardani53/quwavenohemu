Dwight Howard Investigated For Abusing Son, 6 Years Old

Unfortunately, another high-profile sportsman is being accused of abusing a child.

On Nov. 18 it was revealed that Houston Rockets forward Dwight Howard is under an active criminal investigation due to the accusations that he abused his son, Brayson.

The investigation was opened after TMZ revealed the horrifying details of Brayson’s medical examination after his father, Dwight, allegedly used the buckle end of a belt to hit him.

Dwight has reportedly admitted to using the belt on his son, but his excuse for doing so was allegedly that it’s how he was once disciplined as a child, so therefore he did not know that what he was doing was “wrong.” Yikes.

The incident allegedly happened in Georgia, which is where the investigation was opened by the Division of Child Support Services.

There is no word yet on whether or not Dwight is still allowed contact with his son while this investigation is underway.

Dwight’s Legal Team Blames Brayden’s Mom

Of course, the investigation into Dwight’s history of alleged child abuse has sparked a war of words between the basketball player and his son’s mother, Royce Reed.

Dwight’s lawyer claims that Royce is making up the “baseless” accusations against the pro-ball player in order to “extract” money out of him.

“It is appalling to see a mother use her son as a pawn against his father in an attempt to extract money, which is what is happening in this case,” the lawyer told TMZ in an official statement.
