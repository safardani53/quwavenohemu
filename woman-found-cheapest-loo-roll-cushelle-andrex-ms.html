YOU might not have ever put too much thought into your toilet roll before.

But don't worry, one bargain hunter has already done all the hard work for you.

She said: "I've always bought Cushelle, Andrex recently M&S, but saw this in Home Bargains for £9.49 for 32 rolls which is a lot cheaper so gave it a whirl.

"It's not quite as soft, but soft enough i.e. it's not cheap and rough... it's good quality.... so it's what I'll be buying from now onwards."
