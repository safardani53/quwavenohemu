Torrei Hart News, Music, Photos And Videos Hollywood Life

Bio:
Torrei Hart (born Torrei Skipper on Feb. 28, 1978 in North Philadelphia, Pennsylvania) is an American actress, producer, and television personality. Raised in a closely-knit family, Torrei began studying acting and the arts at the young age. She started in numerous plays and productions put on at Winslow Township High School. After graduating, she enrolled in Community College of Philadelphia, where she met fellow actor and comedian Kevin Hart. The two fell in love and relocated to Los Angeles together. As Kevin’s career took off, Torrei pursued her own career, landing national commercials for Toyota, Ebay and parts in various films. In 2012, Torrei launched the YouTube comedy channel PrettyFunnyFish, leading to her developing a large online following. She was also a featured personality on the 2014 VHI reality television show Atlanta Exes. Torrei also has an eye for business. In 2014, she launched her own brand of low-calorie, low-sugar drink mixers. She also is behind Heavenly Hart, a line of natural hair care products. In 2017, she was cast as Melinda in the movie Perfectly Single, alongside Omar Gooding, Joe Torry, and Erica Hubbard.

Best Known For:
Torrei Hart is best known as an actress and for being the ex-wife of Kevin Hart.

Personal Life:
Torrei and Kevin were married in 2003, but they filed for divorce in February 2010, citing irreconcilable differences. The split was finalized in November 2011. The two have tow children together – daughter Heaven Hart and son Hendrix Hart. After the split, both Kevin and Torrei discussed how his infidelity was one of the driving forces behind their breakup.
