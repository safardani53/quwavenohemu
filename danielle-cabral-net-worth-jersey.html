Who is Danielle Cabral from RHONJ?

SEASON 13 of Barvo's Real Housewives of New Jersey is introducing fans to some new faces.

Joining the cast is Danielle Cabral and now fans want to know more about her.

Who is Danielle Cabral from RHONJ?

Danielle Cabral is an entrepreneur and mom of two from Staten Island, New York, known for her over-the-top personality.

She began her career in sales and later founded Boujie Kidz in 2018.

The company is known as an "eccentric clothing company for young children," according to her Bravo biography.

Cabral got the inspiration for the brand by dressing up her kids and was then able to teach herself everything she needed to know about starting a business thanks to YouTube.

“I educated myself on everything,” she told Distractify.

“Like, I stay up late and watch YouTube videos on how to code a website. I taught myself every aspect of a business. And it just snowballed. And then it became my thing. And it brings me joy to do it.

"And it's still very small," she added, "so I'm not coming in like I'm this big, multimillionaire business owner.

"I'm still figuring a lot of stuff out, which you'll get to see on the show. And I'm so excited. Because [for] other women, people want to start a small business, I don't intimidate them.

"It's like, ‘Oh, wait, okay, that's manageable. We could all do it if we wanted to,’ you know, so I'm excited for that."

Cabral will star on RHONJ alongside Teresa Giudice, Melissa Gorga, Margaret Josephs, Dolores Catania, Jennifer Aydin, and Rachel Fuda.

What is Danielle Cabral's net worth?

Cabral joins the RHONJ family with years of experience in the fashion industry.

Her net worth is expected to increase due to her new Bravo role but she has still earned a pretty penny over the years.

At this time, her exact net worth is unclear, but it is reportedly between $100k and $500k.

Is Danielle Cabral married?

When Cabral is not working on building her empire, she can often be found at home, spending time with her family.

Cabral married Flash Security Integrators founder Nate Cabral in 2012 and has since welcomed two kids- son Dominic and daughter Valentina.
