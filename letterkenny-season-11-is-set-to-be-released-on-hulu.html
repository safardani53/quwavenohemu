Letterkenny Season 11 Is Set To Be Released On Hulu

According to CinemaBlend, Letterkenny season 11 will release on Hulu on Monday, December 26, 2022, at 12:01 a.m. ET. The program will premiere on Crave on Sunday, December 25, 2022. The much-anticipated eleventh season will continue to center on fan favorites Squirrelly Dan, Daryl, and others. The series debuted in 2016 and has since received widespread acclaim from both fans and reviewers. It stars Jared Keeso as Wayne, as well as a slew of other actors in key supporting parts.

Letterkenny season 11 on Hulu: Plot, trailer, what to anticipate, and more information revealed

On December 5, 2022, Hulu published the official trailer for season 11 of the program, providing a glimpse inside the show’s colorful universe filled with interesting individuals. It displays a handful of amusing scenes from the forthcoming season in a quick manner without giving away too many critical aspects that may ruin the viewer’s experience. Overall, the clip maintains the show’s decidedly comedic tone and promises lots of chuckles. Along with the teaser, Hulu posted the official season description on their YouTube account, which reads:

“Letterkenny people are divided into three groups: the Hicks, the Skids, and the Hockey Players, who are always arguing over apparently little topics that often end with someone having their ass kicked.”

According to the summary,

“In Season 11, the little town had to deal with the greatest chip varieties, missing pets, an influencer invasion, Skid business, a mystery at the Church Bake Sale, uninvited visitors at the beer league, and the Degens causing havoc. And that’s just to get started.”

Letterkenny Celebrates International Ladies’s Day, a special program shown earlier this year, centered on the women presenting the much-anticipated and hyped Anti-Beauty Pageant. Meanwhile, the following season is likely to center on protagonists Wayne and Katy, among other things, as troubles continue to arise in the quirky little town of Letterkenny. The eleventh season will consist of six episodes, each lasting around half an hour. Chips, Okoya, Lost Dog, N**es, Influenzas, and Degens are the names of the episodes.

A concise rundown of the Letterkenny cast

Jared Keeso plays Wayne in the critically praised comedy. In the season 11 teaser, Keeso looks fantastic, showcasing his particular sense of humor that characterizes the tone of the program. In the following season, viewers can anticipate him to provide another remarkable performance. Keeso is well-known for his roles in 19-2, The Death and Life of John F. Donovan, Shoresy, and Monster Warriors, to mention a few. He has also featured in an episode of the cult classic Supernatural.

Michelle Mylett as Katy, K. Trevor Wilson as Squirrelly Dan, and Dylan Playfair as Reilly, among others, appear in key supporting roles with Jared Keeso. The show’s distinct and eccentric comedy, as well as the performers’ performances, have been acclaimed by critics. It is also regarded as one of the most critically praised comedy of the recent decade. Don’t forget to watch Letterkenny season 11 on Hulu on Boxing Day, Monday, December 26, 2022, at 12:01 a.m. ET.
