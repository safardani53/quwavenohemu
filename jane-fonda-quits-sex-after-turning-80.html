HOLLYWOOD star Jane Fonda has quit sex after turning 80.

Her new movie tells of four OAP pals whose sex lives are rekindled by an erotic novel.

“I am not dating any more. I am 80 and it is only over when you decide it is going to be over.”

Jane, who is three-times divorced, was with record producer Richard Perry, 75, for eight years.

And last year Jane admitted experimenting with sex toys for her role in Netflix comedy Grace and Frankie.

Speaking last year, she said: “We tried all different kinds of vibrators. Use it or lose it, right?”
