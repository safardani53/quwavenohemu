Oops, She Did It Again! Britney Spears Shows Off Her Heinous Extensions! Hollywood Life

No, that’s not a bird’s nest you’re looking at, it’s the back of Britney Spears’ head!

There is one thing in this world that baffles me beyond all things. No, it’s not the question of our existence, or how the universe came to be. No, it is my confusion as to how Britney Spears can be so incredibly wealthy yet have the worst hair in the world. Just look at the back of her head as she was shopping in LA on July 20. I just don’t get it. She could afford to have a new set of extensions put in every day for the rest of her life. Or, just have someone come to her home and blow out her hair daily. Instead, she opts to show off her ratty, unkempt extensions. I myself am actually a huge Britney supporter so it pains me to see her so disheveled. Perhaps one day she’ll clean up her act, but obviously today is not that day.
