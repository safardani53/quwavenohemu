Corey Feldman Takes a Stand TODAY Show Performance

Corey Feldman performed his single, Take a Stand off Angelic 2 the Core on the TODAY Show and absolutely dragged his haters in the classiest way of all.

Kill them with kindness, as they say, which is what he did. 

After Feldman’s Go 4 It performance in October 2016, he received an enormous amount of backlash from fans and critics alike.

At best, haters were calling it the most bizarre performance they’d ever seen, and at worst, were eviscerating Corey on a personal level, insulting his very essence of being. 

Watch as Corey takes his own stand, and maintains his integrity in a way that most of his critics didn’t think possible.
