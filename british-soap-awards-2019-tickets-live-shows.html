British Soap Awards 2019 tickets - are they still available and where is the live show held?

FROM barmy bust-ups to brutal break-ups, our favourite soap stars are always on hand to keep us entertained.

Now, the British Soap Awards, hosted by Phillip Schofield, is finally here to acknowledge the achievements of some of the industry's best loved actors, from Corrie to EastEnders.

Can you get last minute tickets for the British Soap Awards 2019?

Whether you're hedging your best actor bets on Corrie's David Platt, or you're a die-hard Danny Dyer fan, this will not be an event to miss.

So if you want to watch the ceremony live at the venue it's time to buy those last minute tickets for the star-studded evening.

Tickets are still available to purchase online, but they're going fast, so if you want one click here now.

The only seats left (as of May 16) are Rear Upper Circle costing £28.

The awards take place on June 1, 2019.

Follow the Soap Awards on Twitter, Facebook and Instagram for the latest updates.

How much are the tickets for the British Soap Awards 2019?

Ticket prices vary.

They cost from £18.00 for restricted view to £37.50 for circle seats and boxes.

A fee is added to cover administration costs and overheads associated with each ticket sale.

Seats are still available at £28 for the Rear Upper Circle.

Where is the live show of the British Soap Awards held?

This year the star-studded show will be held at The Lowry in Salford.

Last year the ceremony was held at the Hackney Empire, London.

The glitzy event is usually held in either London or Manchester.

Previous venues include The BBC Television Centre (1999-2010) and The London Studios.
