Toulouse vs Liverpool betting preview

Liverpool cruised to a 5-1 victory over Toulouse when the sides met at Anfield a couple of weeks ago and a repeat could well be on the cards on Thursday in France.

Despite Jurgen Klopp making regular changes, Liverpool have sauntered to three wins in the group so far, scoring 10 times and conceding only twice.

A win here would see them reach the knockout stage, although Klopp will be determined to win the group outright – something which comes with the significant advantage.

These days the group winners go straight to the last 16, while the runners-up must play an extra round against one of the Champions League drop-out teams.

Therefore a let-up should not be expected.

Toulouse, who qualified as French Cup winners last season, are enduring a season of struggle.

They sit 14th in Ligue 1, just a point outside the relegation zone, although they have at least accumulated four points in this competition and maintain a decent chance of progressing.

Still, they’ve not won in their last five and the superior quality of Liverpool really should tell.

The Reds have covered a one-goal handicap in all three Europa games so far this season and with the visitors just 2/5 for the win, this looks a good way of backing them at a decent price.

Toulouse vs Liverpool key stats

  • Only Leverkusen (11) have scored more goals in the Europa League group stage so far than Liverpool (10), while only Roma have conceded fewer goals.
  • Toulouse have won only three of their 14 games in all competitions this season and are without a victory in their last five.
  • Mo Salah is already into double figures on the goals front for Liverpool this season. He has 10 in total, including two in the Europa League, despite only starting once in the competition.
  • However, Ben Doak may play ahead of Salah on the right of the Liverpool attack, while the likes of Wataru Endo and Harvey Elliott are set for starts.
  • Toulouse remain without joint top scorer Zakaria Aboukhlal due to a knee injury.
