Amanda Seyfried: I Had &quot;Bigger Breasts&quot; as a Teenager

Amanda Seyfried has mixed feelings about her breasts. Appearing on the cover of Allure‘s May issue, the 27-year-old actress explains why she hasn’t always been satisfied with the size of her chest.

“I saw a picture of them a couple of days ago from when I was 19, and my boobs were way bigger. They were a D, and now they’re a small C, because I lost weight,” Seyfried says. “There was something so beautiful about the size of them. When I look back, I’m like, ‘Why did I always give myself such a hard time?’ Nobody gave me sh-t about it except me.'”

Seyried loves her assets, but she admits they can also pose problems. “They do get in the way a lot,” the Les Miserables actress says. “Like, I wear a really small, tight sports bra when I go running . . . because they want to smack my chin.”

Ironically, Seyfried appears fully-clothed in the magazine’s annual Look Better Naked issue. Though she’s been nude on camera before (in 2006’s Alpha Dog and 2009’s Chloe), the Pennsylvania native says she was initially nervous to play a porn star in the biopic Lovelace. To her surprise, Seyfried says the risque role actually improved her self-confidence.

“Maybe this is backwards, but because she found the power in herself and in her body at a point, it made me feel comfortable in my own skin in a way that I hadn’t been,” Seyfried says of playing Linda Lovelace, who starred in 1972’s X-rated movie Deep Throat in addition to many, many other porn films.”I’ve got parts of me that I wish were tighter, but I don’t kill myself, because it’s not worth it.”
