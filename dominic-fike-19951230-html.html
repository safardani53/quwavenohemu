Dominic Fike (Pop Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Dominic David Fike, is an American singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. Born and raised in Florida, he learned to play the guitar at the age of 10. Fike was first recognized after posting several popular songs on the SoundCloud website. After releasing his first feature film, Don't Forget About Me, Demos, he signed with Columbia Records. Fike's song "3 Nights" reached the top 10 in several countries. Then he collaborated with the group Brockhampton and singer Halsey. In June 2020, Fike released the single "Chicken Tenders". In July of the same year, he released the single "Politics & Violence". Fike's debut album, What Could There Go Wrong, was released the same month. The album reached the top 50 in many countries, including the United States and Australia. In September 2020, Fike represented a series of concerts in the Fortnite video game. A few months later, NME put Fike on their list of new artists needed for 2020.
