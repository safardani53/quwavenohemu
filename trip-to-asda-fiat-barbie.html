How a 5 trip to Asda transformed by pink Fiat 500 into a camper fit for a Barbie doll - it's so co

A YOUNG woman transformed her pink Fiat 500 into a camper fit for a Barbie doll after a £5 trip to Asda.

Hannah explained how she managed to convert the small car into a type of camper van after a trip to the supermarket.

In a TikTok video she showed how she put down a mattress in the back of the 500, with a brushed cotton sheet on top.

Hannah explained that the brushed cotton made the sheet super soft.

She then put up fairy lights in the back of the car with the help of some command strips.

Hannah used some leaf lights from Asda which she said were flexible and suitable for the back of the car.

The lights cost just £50 for a pack of two.

She added: "They work quite well to be fair. I really like them they look really sweet."

Hannah then posted a shot of how pretty the lights looked at night. She also appealed to TikTok users for their thoughts and help.

She added: "It's a work in progress which isn't finished yet. What would you suggest to make a Fiat 500 into more of a "camper"? I'm open to your suggestions"

A car expert has revealed a cheap trick that will save you money and improve your car's safety.

Craig, who works at Walsall Wood Tyre & Service in Walsall, West Midlands, explained via TikTok how to remove condensation from your headlights - which is a common issue for drivers.

The TikToker and car expert recommended popping silica gel packs at the back of the headlights.

Silica gel contains particles that can absorb large amounts of water and are used to protect products from damp conditions.

Drivers can buy these packs for as little as £1.99 from Temu.
However, Craig warns motorists to be careful as the packs could "catch fire" if they get too close to the bulb.
