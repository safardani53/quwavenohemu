Rihanna’s Ever-Changing Beauty Looks

Rihanna showed off this glamorous shot on her Instagram page with the teasing caption, “And that’s a #wrap on my shoot for my new #topsecret add [sic] campaign!!!” Since she’s all decked out in a charm-filled Chanel No. 5 necklace, we have to wonder if RiRi accidentally already let her #topsecret gig out. Is she the latest face of Chanel fragrances? Is that kind of a collaboration even possible?!

With her red nails, red lips and red hair combo, she better be careful that she doesn’t have Chris Brown seeing red! While a red-on-red-on-red combo may not work for all ladies, Rihanna can rock this feisty look. The trick to nailing her mixed reds look is varying the texture and shine of the colors. Her matte red lip contrasts her shiny nails and glossy dark red highlights rather than trying too hard to coordinate the shades.

Rihanna’s short, dark hair is a really flattering look for her. The punch of red in her hair gives her enough of a punk vibe without detracting from her natural beauty. Rihanna’s most successful looks are the ones that showcase her edgy style without crossing the border into trashy.
