Kylie Jenner sneaks into Wonka premiere as beau gets the seal of approval from Kris Jenner

Spotted: Kylie Jenner snuck into the Wonka premiere with mom Kris Jenner to support her new boyfriend, Timothee Chalamet. He’s the lead for the new film, which attracted a flurry of fans to the Los Angeles premiere. Eyes were searching for Jenner – who turned up in shades and a black outfit.

The celebrity couple first sparked dating rumors back in April. Several months later, the two confirmed their status by sharing a steamy smooch in the middle of a Beyoncé’s Renaissance concert at SoFi Stadium in September. Now, Kylie Jenner is doing all she can to support her actor boyfriend, Timothee.

Kylie Jenner spotted at Wonka premiere

Kylie was spotted walking through the crowd surrounded by bodyguards, with her mom Kris Jenner at her side. They both wore slim black shades and black outfits, with Kylie in a sheer long-sleeve and skirt.

During a live audio feed by the fan account Club Chalamet, the account owner said: “Without saying the name of that person, it’s imminent that an announcement will be made because it’s clear as day.”

Kylie skipped the red carpet to support her new beau, but many paparazzi and fans caught photos of her sneaking in. She paired her look with a black suede clutch at the Regency Village Theatre, LA.

Kris Jenner was there to support actor

It’s not just Kylie that was there to support her new man, but her mom, Kris was there too. It looks like Timothee Chalamet and Kylie dating has received the seal of approval from her momager Kris…

Both Kylie and Kris got into a black car while fans bombarded them with cameras. A source told People on Monday Kylie is “incredibly happy” with Chalamet and has even put a label on their romance.

As for the Dune: Part Two actor, the insider said he’s “in awe of everything” The Kardashians star “is accomplishing,” and “especially thinks she is an amazing mom.”

Couple cosily watched Wonka together

Kylie and Timothee cozied up while watching the new movie Wonka, which is out in theaters on Friday. A fan wrote on Twitter that she could see them viewing the film together, after the opening credits.

Some audience members thought it was her sister Kendall Jenner at the premiere, but other fans claim it was “definitely Kylie.” Timothee greeted fans without Kylie before sitting down.
