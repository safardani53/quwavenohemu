Milo Ventimiglia Is Portrayed As Charlie Nicoletti In The Company You Keep

Milo Ventimiglia plays Charlie Nicoletti, a tremendously seductive and intriguing con guy, in ABC’s new drama series The Company You Keep. It is his voyage that is central to the plot. The actor is well-known for his work in television and cinema, including The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, This Is Us, American Dreams, and many more. The show will air on ABC on February 19, 2023.

Milo Ventimiglia has previously been on renowned television programs such as This Is Us and Gilmore Girls.

Milo Ventimiglia was born in Anaheim, California, on July 8, 1977, to Carol and Peter Ventimiglia. He attended El Modena High School in California and apparently spent most of his youth there. Ventimiglia became interested in acting at a young age, appeared in several plays throughout his high school years, and went on to study theater at the University of California, Los Angeles.

After a brief appearance in the short film Gotta Be the Music, Ventimiglia earned another minor part in Will Smith’s The Fresh Prince of Bel Air. Because of its fascinating narrative and performances by the cast, the program gained popularity among viewers and pleased reviewers. Milo Ventimiglia went on to appear in a number of other television programs, including American Dreams, The Bedford Diaries, and Opposite Sex, to name a few. His first notable TV job was in Gilmore Girls when he portrayed Jess Mariano.

He received tremendous appreciation from reviewers and audiences for his role as Jack Pearson in the hit NBC drama series This Is Us, in which he co-starred with Mandy Moore, who portrayed his wife. The role helped him gain huge recognition among TV viewers, and Jack Pearson is still recognized as one of his most memorable television performances by admirers.

Among Ventimiglia’s cinematic acting credits include The Art of Racing in the Rain, Second Act, and the renowned Rocky trilogy, among many more. The actor plays the protagonist in ABC’s new series The Company You Keep. He portrays an intriguing and seductive con guy who falls into a heated romance with a CIA officer. It will be fascinating to observe how his character develops throughout the course of the series.

What is the plot of The Company You Keep? Characters, and further information

The plot of The Company You Keep is about a con guy and a CIA officer whose professional lives get problematic when they become engaged in a love relationship.

According to ABC’s YouTube page, below is the official summary of the show:

“A night of passion leads to love between con artist Charlie an undercover CIA operative Emma, who are unwittingly on a professional collision course. As Charlie builds up the “family company” so he may leave for good, Emma is closing down on the furious criminal who holds Charlie’s family debts in hand, forcing them to confront the falsehoods they’ve lied to in order to rescue themselves and their families from disaster. The show is based on the Korean Broadcasting System’s “My Fellow Citizens.”

Milo Ventimiglia looks fantastic in the clip, effortlessly embodying his character’s gorgeous charisma and secretive nature. Other actors in the supporting cast include Catherine Haena Kim as Emma, Tim Chiou as David, and William Fichtner as Leo, among many others. On Sunday, February 19, 2023, don’t miss The Company You Keep on ABC.
