LOVE Island fans spotted a major clue Molly Marsh will return to the show after being "sent home" in a dramatic re-coupling in tonight's episode.

ITV2 viewers were left shocked as they watched the blonde bombshell suddenly be told to leave when Kady McDermott swiped Zachariah Noble.

In Love Island's most gut-wrenching twist ever, Kady received a text that instructed her to stand in front of the boys at the firepit.

And in scenes that floored fans she picked basketball player Zach Noble, 25, which left devastasted Molly single.

Having picked Zach, Molly also received a text which informed her she was single and "therefore have been dumped from the island".

But viewers noticed that they didn't see Molly packing her suitcase.

Those at home watching spotted that Molly's goodbye hadn't aired either and instead the episode just ended on a major cliff-hanger.

One said: "Sent straight to CASA AMORE maybe?"

Taking to Twitter, another fan wrote: "I don’t think we’ve seen the end of molly …. Too much of a shock ! Watch her return in Casa Amor! Then Zac will have some explaining to do."

Another added: "Doesn't look like Molly has left?!"

A third wrote: "Have a feeling Molly hasn't actually left the villa, we didn't even see her packing right?"

Molly, who was with Zachariah Noble, before being sent packing tonight, was originally coupled up with Mitchel Taylor, 26.

She was also involved in a love triangle with Mitch and Sammy Root, 22, for a few days.

The theatre performer, 21, has been involved in a fair bit of drama since entering the villa on day one.

Viewers ended up reporting the show to broadcasting regular Ofcom after suggesting that Jess Harding, 22, and Ella Thomas, 23, 'bullied' Molly in one scene when she fought for Zach's attention against bombshell Charlotte Sumner.
