Neil Patrick Harris: Naked for Rolling Stone!

Neil Patrick Harris covers the new issue of Rolling Stone, but apparently that’s the only thing the former How I Met Your Mother star has decided to cover.

Yes, NPH went fully nude for the cover of the iconic magazine in a shot taken by controversial photographer Terry Richardson.

Inside the mag, Neil talks about how coming out and Harold & Kumar have both helped his career. 

“Once all the cards were on the table, I got more opportunities than ever,” says Harris about revealing that he’s gay and announcing his engagement to David Burtka. “Some actors don’t get hired because you can’t look into their souls and see what they’re like, because they’re guarded.”

 

Well, the photo above certainly shows us that Neil certainly isn’t guarded.

Asked about his career renaissance in the early 2000s, the former child actor openly credits the blazed adventures of two White Castle enthusiasts, saying, “Who knew three days on a funny stoner comedy would alter my track record the way it did?”

These days, NPH can be seen performing on Broadway in Hedwig and the Angry Inch. 

If you miss Neil as smarmy ladies man Barney Stinson, you can always watch How I Met Your Mother online at TV Fanatic.
