Who is Fedor Emelianenko:

Fedor Emelianenko is a famous MMA Fighter. He was born on September 28, 1976 and his birthplace is Russia. Fedor is also well known as, Russian fighter considered by some to be the greatest heavyweight mixed martial arts fighter of all time; went undefeated for nearly a decade.

Fedor is originated from Russia. He was repeatedly asked to compete in the UFC by UFC president Dana White, who called it an “obsession” to get him to fight in the UFC.
