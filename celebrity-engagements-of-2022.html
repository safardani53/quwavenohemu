Celebrity Engagements of 2022

Caity Lotz & Kyle Schmid

The Legends of Tomorrow actress announced on Instagram May 6 that she and the Big Sky star are getting married.

"I've never been more sure of anything in my life. 💕," Lotz wrote, posting photos showing Schmid down on one knee and their joyful embrace which followed.

Reposting the romantic photos to his own account, Schmid echoed, "The sun rises and sets with you. The moon and the stars have never shone so brightly. You are the love of my life."

Kat Graham & Darren Genet

PEOPLE broke the news May 4 that the director/producer popped the question to the The Vampire Diaries actress during a recent vacation to Mexico.

Graham said yes, with a source telling PEOPLE that "Kat is madly in love and couldn't be happier."

Neither Graham nor Genet have shared more about the moment, and have kept their relationship mostly out of the limelight since they got together.

The couple began dating in 2017 after meeting on set, and have stepped out for a few public appearances together in the years since.

Charlie Barnett & Drew Bender

The Russian Doll star is living happily ever after! Barnett confirmed to Page Six on April 19 that he and his set dresser/designer beau are engaged.

Barnett said that he and Bender already have wedding planning on the brain, envisioning a "very bohemian" theme for their nuptials, set for Wyoming.

"Wyoming is a difficult place to find things. That's where I've been a bridezilla," the actor told Page Six. "Like, where the f--- do I find a porta potty in the vicinity of Salt Lake City? I don't want to have to ship it."

The couple has been romantically linked since June 2018, when the Chicago Fire alumnus posted a photo of Bender to his Instagram page.

The two made their red carpet debut in September 2019 at the Creative Arts Emmy Awards, and hit the 71st Emmy Awards together weeks later.

Isaiah Hartenstein & Kourtney Kellar

The Los Angeles Clippers player celebrated a milestone off the court when he got engaged to Kellar, his girlfriend of three years, soon after the NBA season wrapped.

Posting pictures from the oceanside proposal on Instagram, Hartenstein wrote, "Excited for the next part of our journey!! Love you ❤️💍"

Sharing the same snaps to her own account, the model captioned the carousel in her fiancée's second language of German.

"Ich hab ja natürlich gesagt ❤️," Kellar wrote, which roughly translates in English to, "Of course I said 'yes.' "

The couple began dating in February 2019, and have both cited Valentine's Day as their anniversary in social media posts.

Sofia Richie & Elliot Grainge

She'll be a model bride! Richie announced on Instagram April 20 that she was engaged to Grainge, the founder and CEO of the independent record label 10K Projects.

"Forever isn't long enough," the model wrote of a pair of photos. In the first image, Grainge down on one knee, surrounded by candles before the sea, followed by a closeup of the couple kissing – and Richie's new ring.

Richie took her relationship with Grainge Instagram official in April 2021. At the time, she shared a snap of the pair kissing with a black heart emoji.

Matt Chase & Abbie Beineman

Life was like a country song when the Southerland singer proposed! Chase popped the question to girlfriend Beineman in a romantic outdoor proposal in Arkansas, PEOPLE exclusively reported April 18.

Chase explained that while he had a speech prepared for Beineman, a senior account manager at Futureshirts, it all went out the window as he grabbed her hand and got down on one knee.

"Not sure what I said, but I hope it was sweet," he joked to PEOPLE.

The couple has been together for two years, having connected at a Halloween party after being introduced by a mutual friend.

"This is cliché and a little cheesy to say, but it truly was when you know, you know," the two said in a statement to PEOPLE. "I think that night we knew it was going to be a forever kinda thing."

Madeline Merlo & Chase Fann

In early April, the "It Didn't" singer got engaged to Nashville attorney Fann.

"The day after we met I texted my family and told them that I met my husband last night," Merlo told PEOPLE exclusively. "I've known he was the one since we met and was surprised, but so happy, when he went down on one knee to propose."

She continued, "It's a dream come true."

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck

Lopez and Affleck are getting married!

The couple is engaged, a rep for Lopez confirmed to PEOPLE on April 8. Lopez shared the news in her On the JLo newsletter days after she was photographed wearing her gorgeous new green engagement ring.

She revealed the exciting update in an email to her inner circle. The message to fans included a clip of Lopez admiring a huge, green stone on a silver band on her ring finger, an image of which was also shared by her sister Lynda on social media.

Lopez and Affleck previously began dating in July 2002 and got engaged that November before postponing their September 2003 wedding just days before the date. They ultimately called off their engagement in January 2004.

The Marry Me actress recently told PEOPLE what makes their connection different now compared to 18 years ago, saying, "We're older now, we're smarter, we have more experience, we're at different places in our lives, we have kids now, and we have to be very conscious of those things."

Lopez shares 14-year-old twins Emme and Max with ex-husband Marc Anthony. Affleck shares three kids with ex-wife Jennifer Garner: Violet, 16, Seraphina, 13, and Samuel, 10.

Tia Booth & Taylor Mock

The Bachelor alumna said yes! Booth's boyfriend popped the question during the recent The Bachelor Live On Stage event in Atlanta, she announced on Instagram April 11.

"Never been more shocked or sure in my life💍 I love you so much Tay, my FIANCÉ!!!" Booth wrote, posting a cozy photo with Mock as she flashed her diamond ring.

The physical therapist took her relationship with Mock Instagram official in October 2021, teasing that they had been together for some time before announcing that they were dating.

"Not gonna lie-it's been nice keeping this to myself, but it's about time y'all know too 🖤," she wrote alongside a video of them together.

Booth previously starred on Arie Luyendyk Jr.'s season of The Bachelor in 2018, before she entering a relationship with Colton Underwood during her first time on Bachelor in Paradise. She then dated Cory Cooper from 2018 to 2019 ahead of filming another season of BiP, and left the show single in 2021.

Jack White & Olivia Jean

How punk! The White Stripes rocker proposed to – and married! – the Black Belles frontwoman onstage during a concert on April 8.

White popped the question during a duet of the White Stripes' "Hotel Yorba" just moments before the surprise ceremony at the Masonic Temple in the couple's shared hometown of Detroit.

With his mother Teresa Gillis and Jean's father Brent Markel in the audience, the Grammy winner asked the crowd, "It's been such a great day, mind if we get married right now?"

A rep for White tells PEOPLE "the marriage is legal and valid."

The "Seven Nation Army" singer was previously married to White Stripes bandmate Meg White from 1996 to 2000, and British model Karen Elson from 2005 and 2013. The pair share daughter Scarlett, 15, and son Henry, 14.

Avril Lavigne & Mod Sun

Nothing "Complicated" about it! The pop-punk rocker got engaged to the singer-songwriter in Paris on March 27.

"It was the most perfect, romantic proposal I could ever ask for. We were in Paris on a boat in the Seine River. We had a violin player, champagne and roses," Lavigne told PEOPLE exclusively. "It felt like time stood still, and it was just the two of us in the moment."

The musicians were first linked in February 2021, and made their red carpet debut as a couple at the MTV Video Music Awards in September of last year.

Teaming up in the studio, Lavigne was featured on Mod's 2021 single "Flames," and he co-wrote and co-produced several tracks on her most recent album Love Sux.

Lavigne was previously married to Sum 41 frontman Deryck Whibley from 2006 to 2009, and Nickelback rocker Chad Kroeger from 2013 to 2015.

Nico Santos & Zeke Smith

The TV stars are getting married! The two-time Survivor contestant dropped to one knee at the 2022 GLAAD Media Awards to ask Santos to marry him – and the Superstore actor said yes.

"Nico, your love has taught me how to love. You are my other half, and I want to spend the rest of my life with you," Smith said before proposing on stage, as the crowd went wild.

The moment took the couple's love story full circle. Santos and Smith met four years ago at the 2018 GLAAD Media Awards, and confirmed their relationship later that year.

"I guess everybody knows about my hot little 25 year-old boyfriend now. Oh, haaayyy @nicosantos," Smith wrote on Twitter at the time.

Sinclair Mitchell & Joe Kokofsky

Theatergoers in Providence, Rhode Island, were treated to an extra special "prom-posal" in March.

And now, I want to profess to the world, to your family, to your friends — and some people I don't quite know — that from this day until my last day, if you will have me," Mitchell said.

Trace Bates & Lydia Romeike

The Bringing Up Bates alum announced on Instagram March 31 that he got engaged to his girlfriend– though it took a little planning to surprise Romeike, a photographer!

Bates proposed on the rooftop of the Embassy Suites hotel in Knoxville, Tennessee, asking his sweetheart to marry him in a romantic setting with candles and white rose petals.

Though Romeike was pictured in a turquoise gown in their engagement photos, it wasn't her original outfit. In order to surprise her, Bates explained that he created a fake work event so the photo pro wouldn't be suspicious about heading up to the rooftop.

"I really wanted to surprise Lydia, so I had someone call and 'hire' her to do a photo shoot on the rooftop," he captioned a video clip. "Since I knew she would be dressed in work clothes, I brought her an extra outfit to change into afterward for some classy photos together. The element of surprise meant everything to us, and it all fell into place perfectly."
