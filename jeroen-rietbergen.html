Jeroen Rietbergen Age & Early Life

Jeroen Rietbergen was born on April 19, 1971, in Vlissingen (Flushing), the Netherlands. When he was a toddler, he had an interest in music. Despite his interest in football, baseball, and other outdoor sports when he was ten years old, he chose to study and master the piano. He was more interested in music than in academics. He began his secondary school at the Rotterdam Dance Academy, widely known as Havo for music and dance.

Following that, in 1989, he attended Rotterdam Conservatory. Unfortunately, he did not finish his studies and was forced to quit the institution after just two years. He was expelled because he was less interested in education and spent more time playing keys on various musical instruments. Following this, he abandoned his studies to pursue a career as a musician.
