Who is Neil deGrasse Tyson?

NEIL deGrasse Tyson is often credited as "America's best-known scientist and astrophysicist."

The prolific educator has dedicated his life to exploring the mysteries of our universe.

Who is Neil deGrasse Tyson?

Neil deGrasse Tyson was born on October 5, 1958, in New York City.

Tyson has received degrees from Harvard University, the University of Texas at Austin, and Columbia University.

After receiving his Ph.D., Tyson worked as a postdoctoral research associate at Princeton University.

Additionally, the astrophysicist worked as the director for the Hayden Planetarium in the American Museum of Natural History in 1996.

The following year, he founded the museum's Department of Astrophysics.

Tyson has also hosted several TV series, including Nova, which he appeared in from 2006 to 2011.

In addition to Nova, he hosted the Fox show, Cosmos: Possible Worlds, which premiered on March 9, 2020.

The science commentator is also known for bestselling books such as Astrophysics for People in a Hurry.

Tyson’s professional research interests include star formation, exploding stars, dwarf galaxies, and the Milky Way structure.

In 2001, Tyson was appointed by President George W Bush to serve on a twelve-member commission that studied the Future of the US Aerospace Industry.

Is Neil deGrasse Tyson married?

Neil deGrasse Tyson is married to his wife, Alice Young.

The two tied the knot in 1988, after years of dating during their time together in college.

Tyson and Alice Young met while attending The University of Texas at Austin.

The couple have two children together— a daughter named Miranda and a son named Travis.

Travis Tyson appeared in the second season of Claim to Fame.

Claim to Fame is a guessing game show where contestants have to guess who their celebrity relative is.

On the July 3, 2023, episode, Travis was eliminated as the contestants guessed he was the son of Neil deGrasse Tyson.

What is Neil deGrasse Tyson's net worth?

Neil deGrasse Tyson has been a public figure in the arena of planetary science for a number of years.

Per CelebrityNetWorth, Tyson is worth $5 million.

Apart from being an astrophysicist, author, and television presenter, Tyson has won many awards for his works.
