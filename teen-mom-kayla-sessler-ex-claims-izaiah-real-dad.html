Teen Mom Kayla Sessler's ex Ryan claims he's her son Izaiah's real dad &amp; NOT Stephan Alexander as he

TEEN Mom: Young and Pregnant star Kayla Sessler’s ex Ryan claimed he is her son Izaiah’s real dad and NOT Stephan Alexander.

Ryan demanded a DNA test in a teaser clip for Tuesday’s episode of the MTV show.

The teaser clip began with Kayla, 22, explaining in a voiceover she she’s “so excited” for her fiancé Luke Davis to adopt Izaiah, four.

The Teen Mom star added: "But the other day, I got a weird text from an ex that I haven't heard from in a long time and he's bringing up stuff I thought we had already resolved."

Kayla explained that she and Ryan dated for a couple of months in high school while she was on a break in her relationship with Stephan, who is believed to be Izaiah’s father.

Back when she was pregnant with the four-year-old, Ryan asked if he was the father and she insisted that the baby was Stephan’s.

RYAN SEEKS CONFIRMATION

It seems like Ryan didn’t accept Kayla’s answer, as the MTV star revealed he recently texted her saying he wanted to do a DNA test to confirm he isn't Izaiah’s father.

Kayla said Ryan’s request caught her by surprise and joked she would have taken the opportunity to raise Izaiah with someone other than Stephan if she was able to.

She said: "I don't feel like Ryan's the dad, because if I thought that I would have done that, guys, so long ago.

"I would have done that as soon as Izaiah was born, 'cause Stephan hasn't been s**t from the start.”

Kayla added: “If I thought Ryan was Izaiah's dad, I would have done that a long time ago."

'A LITTLE ANNOYING'

Kayla then slammed Ryan’s request as "a little annoying" since he waited to long to ask about her oldest child’s paternity.

However, she said she’s "still willing to hear what he has to say."

While on the phone with Ryan, he told the Teen Mom: Young and Pregnant star that he's got a "feeling" Izaiah is his son.

Ryan said: "It's just been something that's really been on my mind and kind of really bothering me.”

After he asked for a DNA test, Kayla agreed to meet with her ex to discuss the test further.

In addition to Izaiah, Kayla is also the mother to two-year-old daughter Ariah with Luke.

ROCKY RELATIONSHIP

Fans of Teen Mom: Young and Pregnant have watched Kayla and Stephan's rocky relationship over the years.

During a recent episode of the MTV show, Kayla sat down with her ex's mom before a physical fight ensued.

Security quickly intervened in the physical altercation, though Kayla and Stephan's mother continued to hurl insults at each other.

Stephan has not been a consistent figure in Izaiah's life, while Kayla previously revealed during a Q&A with fans that he has been out of the picture for some time.
