DWTS' Harry Jowsey sparks concern after he shares cryptic post about feeling 'heartbroken' on a 'sad

HARRY Jowsey has sparked concern among fans after posting about having a heartbreaking weekend.

The Too Hot to Handle star took to his Snapchat Story to address a fan who asked why he had been posting any content recently.

Harry, 26, replied to the concerned follower with a zoomed-in photo that showed just a small portion of his face.

The Dancing with the Stars Season 32 contestant appeared to look solemn as he wore his sweatshirt hood over his head.

He responded: "Because it's been a really sad weekend. I'll explain it later. Just a little heartbroken."

Harry, who is still in the running to win this season's Len Goodman Mirrorball Trophy, shared a follow-up Snapchat photo with his dog.

The snap showed him and his DWTS partner, Rylee Arnold, smiling together as Harry held his small black Frenchie in his hands.

While the two were beaming, Harry added a broken heart emoji to the shot, insinuating something bad may have happened to his pup.

On Reddit, fans gave their theories on the reality star's current situation.

"How awful, was this the little puppy that he just got a few weeks ago? So sad," one person wrote.

"Everyone needs to be kind. You never know what people are going through. 'He’s sad about his scores last week' shut UP! Having a pet die is traumatic and extremely heartbreaking. Let’s give him some grace," a second wrote.

While Harry and his partner currently remain in the DWTS competition, the user was referring to the pair receiving the lowest scores from the judges during last week's Most Memorable Year episode.

A third wrote: "This makes me so sad. Say what you want about his dancing but Rylee and Harry have been such a place for lighthearted and fun content but the universe really said let me bring you down with the hate this week and then said let me kick you some more with this."

Others chimed in to give an update on the situation, clarifying that Harry revealed his puppy passed away.

Adding to the online thread, one person wrote: "His new pup passed away. She fell off the couch. So heartbreaking."

"He just posted why, his puppy passed away :( So so sad," a second wrote.

HARRY'S HEARTBREAKING LOSS

Taking to his Snapchat shortly after raising concern among fans, the reality star confirmed his young pup had sadly passed away.

"We lost bean this weekend, when we were about to leave this weekend she fell off the couch and landed flat on her back," he wrote in an updated post.

Harry said he and Rylee raced her to the emergency vet, where they told them it "wasn't looking good for her."

"I made sure they did everything they could to save her," he wrote.

Confirming the tragic news, Harry added: "Yesterday they called me and told me that I had to come say goodbye, so me and Rylee came down and gave her one last kiss and cuddle before she passed."

The heartbreaking update was paired with an adorable photo of Harry and his dog as she peaked her head out from behind his head.

Despite some viewers believing Harry is only on the show "for PR and Marketing purposes," he will have another chance to impress the judges and gain votes from fans.
