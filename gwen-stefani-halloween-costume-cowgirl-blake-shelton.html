Cute Country Cowgirl For Blake Shelton? Hollywood Life

No matter what she’s wearing, Gwen always looks absolutely flawless. The “Used to Love You” singer rocked a colorful cowgirl getup for the Casamigos Tequila Halloween party, and added a little something extra to the ensemble: a healthy smattering of blood! Hey, it’s Halloween, after all. You might as well get spooky while you can.
