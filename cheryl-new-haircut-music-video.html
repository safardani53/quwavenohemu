Cheryl reveals dramatic new short haircut in promo video for her new single

CHERYL has gone for a much shorter sexy hairdo for her comeback single.

In the promo for Love Made Me Do It, the stunning singer wears her hair in a chic, sexily-tousled bob.

Staring away into the distance, Cheryl's super-shiny tresses sit just above her shoulders and are a dramatic change from her usual long, flowing style.

Cheryl loves to experiment with her hair and over the years has tried out many different styles including corn rows, a graduated bob, extensions and even a beehive.

Known for her classic long, tumbling locks, the trendsetter is going for a more grown-up look with this shorter style.

The choppy length suits Cheryl's petite features and the texture frames her face perfectly.

Eagle-eyed fans also spotted the former X Factor judge's new shorter hair in the promo for her interview with singer Jessie Ware's podcast, Table Manners.

In the interview, Cheryl spoke candidly about how she was feeling after a four-year break from the music scene.

During the hiatus, which Cheryl has said she needed in order to save her sanity, the ex-Girls Aloud star got together with former One Direction member Liam Payne and gave birth to their first child.

Since the couple sadly split back in July this year, fans have been eager to hear Cheryl's side of things.

And now it seems they won't have long to wait.

Teasing her new single, the singer revealed a telling lyric from her song: "Oh my god, I’m such a sucker, I fall in love with every f***er".

The 35-year-old also sings: "Love made me do it... and I'd do it all again."

Speaking about her latest venture, Cheryl said: "It’s something that anyone who’s been in love will be able to relate to in some way or another.

"It’s not about one specific person or relationship, it’s more about me being the lover of love that I am. I just love love!”
