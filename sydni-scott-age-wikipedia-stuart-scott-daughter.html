How Old Is Stuart Scott Daughter?

Sydni Scott Age: Stuart’s daughter, Sydni, is presently 24 years old.

She was 15 years old, while her sister Taelor was 19 when their father, Stuart, passed away in 2015.

Stuart Scott (July 19, 1965 – January 4, 2015) was a prominent American sportscaster and anchor on ESPN.

He is best known for his long tenure as a co-anchor on ESPN’s flagship program, SportsCenter.

Scott joined ESPN in 1993 and quickly became known for his unique and energetic broadcasting style.

Some of his famous catchphrases include “Boo-yah!”, “Hallah,” and “As cool as the other side of the pillow.”

He battled appendiceal cancer for several years before his death on January 4, 2015, at the age of 49.

Stuart Scott Wife & Children

Stuart Scott married Kimberly Scott in 1993, and they had two daughters, Taelor and Sydni.

However, their marriage ended in divorce in 2007 after 14 years.

At the time of his passing, Stuart Scott was in a relationship with Kristin Spodobalski.

Kristin graduated with a bachelor’s degree in business administration and management from Saint Joseph College in 2009.

She has been the Vice President of Sales at Wellthy since November 2023.

Sydni Scott: Age & Wikipedia

Stuart’s daughter, Sydni, is presently 24 years old.

She attended Miss Porter’s School before earning her bachelor’s degree in political science and government from Columbia University, where she actively participated in track and field.

During her third year at Columbia, Sydni founded The Amendment Project, a student-led grassroots initiative to foster discussions on reparative justice in American political discourse.

Furthermore, she was selected as a member of the inaugural class of the Saltzman Student Scholar Fellowship of the School of International and Public Affairs.

To gain insights into international aspects of racial politics, Sydni interned at the Atlantic Fellows for Racial Equity, National Diversity Equity and Inclusion, the Council on Foreign Relations, and the Global Sports Mentoring Program.

Currently, she is pursuing an MPhil at the University of Oxford as a Rhodes Scholar. Her future plans include enrolling in law school after completing her studies at Oxford.

Elder Sister: Taelor

Sydni’s sister, Taelor Scott, completed her education at Kingswood Oxford School and obtained a bachelor’s degree in anthropology from Barnard College.

Taelor is a filmmaker, director, and producer who has written and directed three short films: “Mod Squad and the Spectacular Paper Caper,” “Baltimore Whoosh” and “The Ballad of Chocolate Mabbie.”

Subsequently, she has engaged in freelance academic tutoring and worked as a photography assistant.

Currently, Taelor serves as the library coordinator for Raedio in Los Angeles, California.

Additionally, both the Scott sisters serve as ambassadors for The V Foundation.
