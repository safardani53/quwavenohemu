Who is Jon Gordon:

Jon Gordon is a famous Self-Help Author. He was born on January 20, 1971 and his birthplace is Long Island, NY. Jon is also well known as, Wall Street Journal bestselling author and keynote speaker whose titles include The Energy Bus, Soup, The No Complaining Rule, Training Camp, and The Carpenter. His books cover topics of leadership, culture, sales and teamwork.

Jon is originated from United States. He was associated with Mike Smith when he was the head ball coach for the Atlanta Falcons, another one of his clients.
