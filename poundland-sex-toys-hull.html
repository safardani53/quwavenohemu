Britain's most buzzing city is Hull as more Poundland sex toys have been bought there than anywhere

BRITAIN’S most buzzing city is Hull after it emerged more Poundland sex toys were bought there than anywhere else.

The cheap £1 thrill sex toy range called Nooky was launched last month by the bargain chain and shoppers have been left thrilled.

According to Poundland bosses the largest amount of gadgets — more than 47,000 — were sold in Hull, which is also Britain’s City of Culture.

Bosses said the range of sex toys, which include vibrating love rings and finger fun stimulators, were selling at the rate of one a second.

Hull was followed by Sheffield and Milton Keynes. Lowest sales were in Nuneaton, Warks, where only 90 were sold.

Poundland's trading controller Chris Burns said: “We’ve been thrilled by the demand for the new range across the UK.

We are currently delivering thousands of additional items to stores who’ve sold out, so couples can continue to get their Nooky wherever they live.”

Sex expert Dr Pam Spurr said: "Poundland's the perfect place to spice up your sex life — and save pounds."
