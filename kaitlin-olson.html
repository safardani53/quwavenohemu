Kaitlin Olson Age & Biography

Kaitlin Olson was born on August 18, 1975 in Portland, Oregon, USA, and is the only child of Donald Lee Olson (father) and Melinda Olson (mother). (mother). Her father was the publisher of the Portland Tribue from 2000 to 2001, and her mother was a nurse.

Her parents gave her the name Kaitlin Willow Olsen at birth. She moved with her family to Spokane, Washington, shortly after her birth, and then to Vashon Island, Washington, in Puget Sound, 13 miles (21 kilometers) west of Seattle, where she lived until the age of eight. Her family later returned to Portland, settling in Tualatin, where Olson grew up on a farm. Her nationality is American, and she is of mixed ethnicity, with Welsh, Irish, German, Swedish, and English ancestors.

Olson attended Tigard High School in Oregon for her education. She then went to the University of Oregon. She earned a Bachelor of Science in theatre arts from the university in 1997. She moved to Los Angeles after college to pursue a career as an actress. Kaitlin also performed with The Groundlings Sunday Company. During her early career, she even toured with the USO in Bosnia, Kosovo, and Norway.
