Who Killed the Shock Jock?

Last night’s Bones Season 10 Episode 3 showed the Jeffersonian team trying to move on from the tragic death of Sweets, which of course proved difficult.

There was a new case and a new investigation to distract them, however.

Watch Bones Season 10 Episode 3 Online

Booth’s behavior in particular is cause for concern for Brennan, who feels like he’s avoiding and repressing his emotions in Bones Season 10 Episode 3.

Ironic that Brennan is concerned about someone who does so? You bet.

She’s not wrong though, and given that he’s been in prison and then lost such a special, valued person in his life, she feels there could be more dire consequences.

Basically, no one is safe from Booth’s suspicions and short tempter.

Everyone is out to get him, save for the squints who simply don’t have enough power, which doesn’t bode well for new team member Aubrey. Poor Aubrey.

Sure, he might seem a little annoying, but Booth is going out of his way to treat him as such, it seems, even as he seeks an active role in the case of the week.

 

If you watch Bones online today, you’ll see Booth ultimately agree to let Aubrey to follow him around as they probed the death of a local radio personality.

Fortunately, Sweets would have approved as Booth did lean on his new partner, along with his wife of course, in figuring out who killed the shock jock.

This guy was so conservative, he would make … [insert real life conservative media personality] here blush. He got 100 angry letters per day. Per DAY.

The suspect was not liberal, in the end, but a fellow conservative, a white supremacist not as good at fighting as he was at making handmade bombs.

Nor was he their killer, either.

The host loved visiting a dominatrix, and was in fact a moderate who said things that made him feel like he needed help, and then wanted off the show.

As such, the producer stood to lose a lot of money. But he didn’t do it either, merely moving the body after finding Hutch in full S&M gear and … panicking.

The killer was Hutch’s partner on the radio. He planned on blackmailing him to stay on the radio and a fight broke out, with the death being an accident.

And that was all she wrote.
