Lauren Hashian Poses with Dwayne Johnson at Hercules Premiere: See the Pics!

Dwayne Johnson came out for the premiere of Hercules last night, posing often for photographers at the TCL Chinese Theatre in Los Angeles.

This came as no surprise, of course. Johnson is the star of the movie and all.

But the WWE star-turned-actor was accompanied for the event by Lauren Hashian, as Johnson’s long-time girlfriend made a rare public appearance.

Johnson met Hashian while filming The Game Plan in 2007. He divorced wife Dany Garcia a year later, with many speculating that an affair with Hashian was to blame.

But Johnson and Garcia remain close, with the latter even working alongside her ex-husband on his movie career, so whatever transpired appears to have been long forgotten by both sides.
