Hollywood Hillbillies' Delores Hughes dead at 76 - beloved reality star dies of heart failure

HOLLYWOOD Hillbillies star Delores Hughes has passed away at the age of 76.

The reality star died of heart failure at a hospital in Grayson, Georgia, last Wednesday, TMZ reported.

Delores' manager, David Weintraub, broke the heartbreaking news to the outlet, sharing kind words about the beloved star.

"Mema was the greatest, most outspoken 'tell-it-like-it-is personalities the world had ever seen on TV. I know that she will still be bossing all of us around from heaven," he said.

Delores is survived by her husband, Paul Conlon, daughter Dee Dee Peters, son John Cox, and grandson and YouTube star, Michael Coppercab Kittrell.

The Hollywood Hillbillies followed the hilarious antics of Delores, also known as Mema, and her family while living in the countryside in Georgia.
