MORE ON GIGS AND TOURS

Who are S Club 3?

S Club 3 are three members of the original S Club 7 pop group - Jo O'Meara, Bradley McIntosh and Tina Barrett.

Jo and Bradley with Paul Cattermole reformed as a trio in 2008.

Tina then joined the band in 2014 and they played various gigs as a four-piece.

In 2015 all seven member returned for their sell-out Bring It All Back reunion tour and in 2017 S Club 3 announced they were planning a world tour.
