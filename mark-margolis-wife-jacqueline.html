Who is Mark Margolis' wife, Jacqueline?

MARK Margolis is well-known for his role in the hit TV show, Breaking Bad.

Following his tragic passing, fans want to know more about the actor's wife, Jacqueline.

When did Mark and Jacqueline Margolis get married?

Mark and Jacqueline Margolis were married for over 60 years.

The couple wed on June 3, 1962.

Mark has now passed away at the age of 83, as per The Hollywood Reporter.

According to the outlet, the couple's son — actor and Knitting Factory Entertainment CEO Morgan H. Margolis — announced that the TV star died on Thursday, August 3, 2023, at Mount Sinai Hospital in New York City following a brief illness.

Which movies did Jacqueline Margolis act in?

Like her late husband, Jacqueline also pursued an acting career.

According to her IMDb profile, she has been featured in multiple movies, including Summer of Sam (1999) and Death in Love (2008).

The actress also appeared in the short films City Hall (2003) and Mental Health (2013).

Jacqueline's page also states that she was born on November 22, 1939, in Queens, New York.

According to her Facebook account, @jacqueline.margolis, she attended Far Rockaway High School and the University of Miami.

Do Mark and Jacqueline Margolis have kids?

Mark and Jacqueline have one kid together.

Their son, Morgan, was born in New York City in 1966.

Like his parents, the 57-year-old has pursued an acting career.

According to his IMDb page, he has been featured in a number of television shows and films and is known for III Met by Moonlight (1994), Rip It Off (2001), 3000 Miles to Graceland (2001), and Dexter (2006).

He is also known for appearing in Star Trek: Voyager (1998) and Star Trek: Enterprise (2002).

In addition to his acting career, Morgan became the CEO of Knitting Factory Entertainment in 2008.

The company's website states: "Since its inception in 1987, Knitting Factory Entertainment ('KFE' or the 'Company') has grown from a single New York City venue into a comprehensive leader in the music and entertainment business.

"Today, the Company’s operations include venue ownership and management, festivals and events, touring, artist management, recorded music production and distribution, and other initiatives around the Knitting Factory brand and audience.

"Spanning across the U.S. with offices and venues in New York, Spokane, Boise, and Los Angeles, KFE is active across the United States in all markets."

Morgan's IMDb page also states that he graduated from the High School of Performing Arts and that he is married to a woman named Heidi Blose. The pair share three children.

The family lives in Los Angeles, California.
