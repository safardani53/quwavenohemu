Who is in the cast of Evil Dead Rise?

WARNER Brothers' newest horror film tells the "twisted tale" of two estranged sisters who come together to fight flesh-possessing demons.

The film comes from the mind of Lee Cronin and includes a star-studded cast.

Who is in the cast of Evil Dead Rise?

The cast of Evil Dead Rise is led by Alyssa Sutherland and Lily Sullivan, who portray the roles of sisters Ellie and Beth.

Other cast members include:

  • Mirabai Pease as Teresa
  • Richard Crouchley as Caleb
  • Anna-Maree Thomas as Jessica
  • Noah Paul as Bruce
  • Gabrielle Echols as Bridget
  • Morgan Davies as Danny
  • Nell Fisher as Kassie
  • Billy Reynolds-McCarthy as Jake
  • Tai Wana as Scott
  • Jayden Daniels as Gabriel
  • Mark Mitchinson as Mr. Fonda
  • Melissa Xiao as a newscaster

Is Evil Dead Rise a sequel?

While Evil Dead Rise might be new to some horror fans, it's actually connected to a beloved supernatural horror trilogy that was created by Sam Raimi.

Read More from film

Evil Dead Rise is a sequel to Evil Dead (2013), which was a soft reboot of the original 1980s trilogy, according to Variety.

The original film, The Evil Dead, was released in 1981 followed by its two sequels, Evil Dead II (1987) and Army of Darkness (1993).

“The ‘Evil Dead’ movies filled my brain with terror and awe when I first saw them at 9 years old,” Cronin said in a statement.

“I am excited and humbled to be resurrecting the most iconic of evil forces for both the fans and a whole new generation.”

While Cronin might've been inspired by the original films, he has put his own gory twist on this one and even used over 1,700 gallons of blood.

“We used 6,500 liters [1,717 gallons] of blood on the movie,” Cronin revealed, via Variety.

“That is real, sticky, cooked movie blood. We had to hire out this industrial kitchen to cook the blood and keep it fresh, be able to heat it up, because characters are covered in it.

"There was a lot of management of liquid in this movie. I wanted blood to be a character, so it was important we got the viscosity and look just right.”

How can I watch Evil Dead Rise?

Evil Dead Rise will make its theater debut in the United States on April 21, 2023.

Later on, the film will be available to stream on HBO Max with a valid subscription.
