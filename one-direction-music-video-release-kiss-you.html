One Direction Music Video Release: &quot;Kiss You&quot;

The teasing is over. The full-length cuteness has finally hit the Internet.

Following days’ worth of brief online teases, One Direction has finally released the music video for "Kiss You."

It features the young British men driving a motorcycle and convertible, while also donning outfits in jail and staging a photoshoot in ski garb.

Does it make a great deal of sense? Not really. Will fans swoon over lyrics that include "if you don’t wanna take this slow/if you just wanna take me home/let me say yeah a yeah a yeah yeah a yeah / and let me kiss you?" We’d imagine so…

NOTE: No word on whether or not Harry Styles penned this track specifically for Taylor Swift.

But those two are adorable together, don’t you think?
