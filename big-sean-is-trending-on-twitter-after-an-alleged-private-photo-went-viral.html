Big Sean Is Trending On Twitter After An Alleged Private Photo Went Viral

Big Sean was recently trending on Twitter after a purported private photo of the rapper circulated on social media. According to The Shade Room, the rapper published the now-deleted picture on Instagram for his close pals, but it was inadvertently made public. Several others made amusing parallels of the image to the Nintendo Switch and even mentioned the musician’s girlfriend, Grammy-nominated singer Jhene Aiko.

Sean addressed the incident and denied the claims after the photo went viral on social media. The singer responded to The Shade Room’s Instagram post, clarifying that the supposed picture was not his:

“This is not me,”

The new internet scandal comes only days after Sean and Aiko posed for a romantic photo backstage at the Super Bowl before the latter’s performance.

“All of a sudden, I’m a big admirer of his” – Twitter responds to Big Sean’s claimed viral picture.

Big Sean, actual name Sean Michael Leonard Anderson, made headlines lately when a purported intimate picture of the artist went viral on social media. Despite the rapper’s rapid dismissal of the accusations and clarification that the image did not belong to him, netizens continued to respond to the matter with humorous memes and amusing comments. As internet comments continue to stream in, it remains to be seen if Sean will address the subject again in the coming days.

Regarding his professional career, the rapper recently worked with Queen Naija on their new track, Hate Our Love. During an Instagram live promotion of the song, Sean also revealed that he is now working on a new album with his girlfriend, Jhene Aiko:

“I’m here with Jhene working on [a] new album and new TWENTY88… We’ve been working on it for a short time… But it’s coming up well.”

In 2016, the duo cooperated for the first time on their successful EP TWENTY88. That same year, the couple announced their connection. The couple split up temporarily in 2018 but have apparently reunited and are again dating.
