VS Angel Martha Hunt Reveals Jennifer Lopez is her Body Goals

Even VS Angels say J.Lo is the definition of body goals! Victoria’s Secret Angel Martha Hunt reveals exactly how she is sculpting her booty to get runway ready for the lingerie brand’s upcoming November show.

“Operation Jennifer Lopez booty is in full effect,” the 29-year-old North Carolina native revealed to Us Weekly at an Xtend Barre workout for the Victoria’s Secret Sport launch in New York City on Wednesday, September 18.

“Leading up to the show, I am amping it up. I have been very glute-focused this year because I set a personal fitness challenge for myself to do 10 consecutive squats with really great form. I know that doesn’t sound that challenging but my squats just aren’t my expertise,” the Free People spokesmodel shared. “I have bad form with squats so I do a few, usually three sets of 10 squats, [because] I want to see the progression from the beginning to the end.”

The model, who has scoliosis, a condition that causes a sideways curvature of the spine, also swears by Xtend classes, a barre-based workout that is meant to help lengthen the body and perfect posture while strengthening head-to-toe. “I feel taller afterwards,” she says. “I think anything that you can do that makes you feel good, stronger and it helps you stretch, is great for scoliosis.”

Hunt insists that although the Shades of Blue star’s booty is inspiring her workouts, it’s not just the World of Dance judge’s physical assets that fuel her girl crush.
