Rick Smith Jr (Magician) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Richard Smith Jr is a professional magician and card thrower from Cleveland, Ohio. Smith is known for his many performances on national television as well as three world records for throwing cards over the greatest distance, the greatest altitude and the most accurate. Drawing on his throwing skills from his days as a handball player with the Division I NCAA at Cleveland State University, he has also released a number of educational films about magic and card-throwing practice.
