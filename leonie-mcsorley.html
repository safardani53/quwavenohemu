Who is Leonie McSorley? Demi Sims ex-girlfriend was on Ex on the Beach! Mon 18 January 2021

Demi Sims and Leonie McSorley have split up after Demi went Instagram official with new girlfriend Francesca Farago. 

The TOWIE star has made her relationship with Francesca Farago Instagram official, confirming the exciting news that they are dating. 

Demi’s followers were thrilled to learn about her new romance but many want to find more information about her ex-girlfriend Leonie.

So, who is Demi’s ex Leonie McSorley?

About Leonie McSorley

Leonie McSorley is a 22-year-old model and reality star from Aberdeen, Scotland. She also has a twin sister called Ché who models and has appeared on the same shows. Leonie and Ché now both live in Manchester.

Back in 2015, the twins made their reality TV debut on Geordie Shore. They briefly appeared on the show in season 13 episode 2 for a night of passion in a caravan with lead stars Scotty T and Gaz Beadle.

It was when Leonie and Ché appeared on MTV’s other hit series Ex on the Beach that they really made a name for themselves as reality stars.

To keep up to date with Leonie you can follow her on Instagram, where already she has over 93,000 followers. Find Leonie @leonie_mcsorley.

Leonie McSorley on Ex on the Beach

Leonie signed up to the MTV reality show with Ché. The twins appeared on the 2017 series and were some of the most talked-about participants that summer.

Notably, Leonie was the subject of some cruel trolling about a skin condition she suffered from. Leonie explained that she got a skin condition from sunbeds that affected her pigment.

The trolling became so bad that Leonie addressed it on Twitter in August 2017.

Leonie and Demi Sims’ relationship

It is unconfirmed when exactly Leonie got together with TOWIE star Demi. The couple have publicly displayed their feelings on social media. Demi commented on one of Leonie’s Instagrams with a love heart on April 13th, 2020 so it’s clear the couple had been speaking before then.

Although Demi announced the couple were official on June 20th, Leonie had actually announced it to her Instagram followers the day before. They both used the same couple photo. In Leonie’s post, she captioned: “Wifed off”

When they became Instagram official, the two have not shied away from PDA. In one Instagram post of Leonie’s, Demi commented “THATS MY BABY,” with plenty of heart-eyed emojis.

But their romance is in the past now since Demi has moved on with her new girlfriend, Too Hot to Handle star Francesca Farago.
