Davor Šuker is a former Croatian professional footballer and current president of the Croatian Football Association, a position he has held since July 2012. During his playing career, Šuker was a striker. He started his home footballing career for local first team NK Osijek at the age of 16. In his last season with the club, he became the league's top scorer. He moved on to signing from Dinamo Zagreb in 1989. The war for independence in Croatia interrupted a promising season for the 21-year-old, which ultimately led Šuker to join Spanish club Sevilla. regular performance with Sevilla and still among the tournament's top scorers. He signed for Real Madrid five years later and was once again among the tournament's top scorers. At the Santiago Bernabéu, he also helped Madrid win the La Liga and the UEFA Champions League.
