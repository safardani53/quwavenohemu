Who is Joyce Didonato:

Joyce Didonato is a famous Opera Singer. She was born on February 13, 1969 and her birthplace is Kansas. Joyce is also well known as, American mezzo-soprano who has performed with some of the world’s most renowned opera companies, including the New York City Opera and Orchestre National de France. She was awarded the Grammy for Best Classical Vocal Solo for her album Diva Divo in 2012.

Joyce is originated from United States. She performed in Pulitzer Prize winner David Lang’s Modern Painters in 1994.
