INTROVERTS can breathe easy thanks to a side hustle aficionado who's shared three gigs that start at $24 per hour with little effort.

Cooper, who goes by the username coopergknee on TikTok, recently posted a video explaining specific jobs you could take on that provide a steady stream of passive income.

They can pair well with a full-time job or even serve as a nice change from something that is costing you too much time and energy.

1. Netflix binge watcher

The first "lazy" job opportunity Cooper pointed out is a "Netflix Binge Watcher" that pays about $24 per hour as compensation.

This does actually happen, and people can be paid to watch Netflix, except it's not the streaming service that is paying them to do so.

The job ad Cooper showed is likely for a separate company that has a specific reason for needing someone to watch Netflix to promote or test their product.

For example, a contact lens company offered $70,000 earlier this year for someone to watch 10 different Netflix shows to test and rate their product, reports PennLive.

The Sun found several "Netflix Binge Watcher" ads online, with the hourly rates ranging up to $33.

2. Ice cream taster

Coming in second on the list of Cooper's lucrative side hustles for little effort is an ice cream taster.

According to the advertisement listing she showed, the company offers just over $48,000 per year salary to taste ice cream.

Again, on the surface level, this seems simple and sounds amazing, which has some truth to it.

However, this position likely requires some kind of college-level degree in food science or a related field, as the ice cream tasters truly evaluate the product.

According to Indeed, it is an in-demand profession, although it requires some business skills as well.

The site explained: "It requires creativity to invent new concoctions that have the right texture and a desire to experiment with taste combinations that customers like.

"Ice cream tasters usually have skills such as patience, imagination and a discerning palate to evaluate different flavors."

3. Fortune cookie writer

Lastly, Cooper explained that there is a demand for fortune cookie writers.

Someone does have to come up with the inscribed messages that customers crack open at the heart of the cookie.

The job ad Cooper showed has the salary for a fortune cookie writer at about $60,000, which many would likely consider a solid living.

This is certainly a fun and meaningful writing opportunity for a creative thinker but requires some skills and awareness to be truly effective.

You have a better chance of landing a position like it with some professional writing experience or a detailed writing portfolio.

Also, the demand for this position isn't terribly high, as many fortune cookie writers often start their own companies, according to Indeed.
