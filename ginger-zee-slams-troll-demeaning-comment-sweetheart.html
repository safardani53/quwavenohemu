Ginger Zee slams troll over demeaning comment and demands dont call me that! as fans

GINGER Zee has slammed an online troll for calling her "sweetheart" in a demeaning reply to one of her tweets.

The GMA meteorologist had spent some time reporting on some of the sweltering temperatures across the country in a set of recent social media posts.

She took the time to point out some of the cities that had been feeling the heat the most.

Temperatures have been soaring over the past few weeks in several places.

But this apparently annoyed some viewers, who decided to troll Ginger.

One decided to tweet at Ginger directly, pointing out that "it's summer" and to expect hot temperatures.

He also called her "sweetheart" in his acerbic response.
