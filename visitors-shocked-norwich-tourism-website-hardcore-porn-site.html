Web users shocked after Norwich tourism site accidentally redirects them to hardcore porn site T

A TOURISM webpage to promote Norwich directed visitors to a hardcore porn site.

Taxpayer-funded visitnorwich.co.uk mistakenly linked its blog on pancakes to a kinky sex advice site.

Visitors wanting to browse menus from mobile caterer Christophe’s Crepes were instead sent to Christophe’s, a website with graphic images of sex and threesomes.

It offers advice on spicing up sex lives and appears to be aimed at men.

One diner was shocked by the link as he sought somewhere to eat in the Norfolk city — home of East Anglian TV comedy character Alan Partridge.

The man said: “I couldn’t believe it. My eyes almost popped out of my head. I was expecting to see plenty of bananas and cream — but only as part of a pancake menu.”

Christophe’s Crepes boss Chris Smith said: “We apologise if this caused any offence. We knew nothing about it.”

The pancake page containing the link had yesterday been removed.

Visit Norwich — which is partly funded by Norwich city council — failed to respond to requests for comment.

Its directors include dean of Norwich Cathedral, the Very Rev Jane Hedges.

The city council insisted it had nothing to do with the website.
