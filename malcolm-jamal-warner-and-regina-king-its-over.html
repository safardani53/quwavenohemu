Malcolm-Jamal Warner and Regina King: It's Over!

Malcolm-Jamal Warner and Regina King have called it quits, apparently.

The couple dated for two years, it wasn’t the most amicable of breakups.

“He said he wasn’t feeling it anymore,” a source adds, explaining that Warner asked King and her young son (with ex-husband Ian Alexander Jr.) to move out.

The SouthLAnd star is “still very brokenhearted” over it, too.

 

Friends sinc both were child TV stars – Warner on The Cosby Show and King on 227 – the twosome, both 42, rarely opened up about their romance.

The couple shot down engagement rumors in 2011; King divorced Ian Alexander Sr. after a decade of marriage back in 2007. Warner has never wed.
