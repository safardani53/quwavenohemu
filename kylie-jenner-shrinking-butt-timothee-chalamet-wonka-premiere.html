Kylie Jenner reveals her shrinking butt in skintight leggings as she sneaks into Timothee Chalamet's

KYLIE Jenner has been spotted secretly attending a major event to support her boyfriend, Timothée Chalamet.

The two A-list celebs were first rumored to be dating back in April but confirmed their relationship by showing off major PDA at a Beyonce concert in September.

The mother-daughter duo were photographed secretly leaving the premiere by sneaking out the back door to avoid the paparazzi.

Kylie stunned in an all-black ensemble which included skintight leggings that showed off her shrinking backside.

On top, the make-up mogul wore a sheer long-sleeved shirt and accessorized her look with a pair of thin black sunglasses with her luscious black hair let down.
