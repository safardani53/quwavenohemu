Kristen Bell Hollywood Life

Bio:
Kristen Bell (born on July 18, 1980 in Huntington Woods, Michigan) is an American actress. After appearing on Broadway in the early 2000s, Kristen made it big with the lead role in Veronica Mars, a CW show with an extremely loyal cult following. When the show ended in 2007, Kristen joined the cast of Heroes and also played the off-screen narrator of Gossip Girl. She made the jump to the big screen in 2008 as Sarah Marshall in Forgetting Sarah Marshall. Kristen has since starred in a number of other movies such as Couples Retreat and When In Rome. She began starring in Showtime’s House of Lies in 2012. A Veronica Mars movie is set to be released in 2014.

Best Known For:
Kristen Bell is best known for playing Veronica Mars on Veronica Mars.

Personal Life:
Kristen Bell was born and raised in Huntington Woods, Michigan. She dated Kevin Mann for five years between 2002 and 2007. Shortly after they broke up, Kristen started dating actor Dax Shepard. The couple was engaged in 2010 and got married in 2012. Kristen had her first child with Dax in March 2013, a daughter named Lincoln.
