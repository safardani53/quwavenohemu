Rod Wave tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

RAPPER Rod Wave announced he is going on tour in the summer of 2022.

Rod Wave has been in the limelight since he dropped his first album in November 2019.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

When is Rod Wave's 2022 tour?

Rod Wave, whose real name is Rodarius Marcell Green, will be dropping his third album ahead of his 2022 tour.

The tour is set to begin on June 10, 2022, in Lexington, Kentucky, and will extend through July 3.

He posted the tour list on his Instagram and said he will be announcing a second tour in August 2022.

His tour will begin only days after his new album, Beautiful Mind, is scheduled to be released on June 3.

The first album Rod Wave released, Ghetto Gospel, reached the no. 10 spot on Billboard's Top 200, and his second album released in 2020, Pray 4 Love, peaked at the no. 2 spot on Billboard's Top 200.

Rod Wave discussed his style of music with Montreality in August 2021 and said, “I could make club songs. I could make bangers and shit that don’t make no sense. I could make that shit all day.

"… I don’t wanna feed nobody no bullshit. I could tell you about pounds, I could tell you about trappin’, I could tell you about stealin’ and robbin’ and killin’, I could do that.

"It’s just that I don’t wanna do that. It’s a new trapper-rapper every day.”

Where is Rod Wave touring?

Rod Wave's first 2022 tour will take place at the following locations:

  • June 10 at Rupp Arena (Lexington, Kentucky)
  • June 11 at Cross Pointe Park (Chicago, Illinois)
  • June 17 at Chesapeake Arena (Baltimore, Maryland)
  • June 18 at Vystar Arena (Jacksonville, Florida)
  • June 19 at Chartway Arena (Norfolk, Virginia)
  • June 24 at Liacouras Center (Philadelphia, Pennsylvania)
  • June 25 at Crown Coliseum (Fayetteville, North Carolina)
  • June 26 at Bon Secours Arena (Greenville, South Carolina)
  • July 2 at Lil Ceasar's Arena (Detroit, Michigan)
  • July 3 at T Mobile Center (Kansas City, Missouri)

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for Rod Wave's tour have gone on sale on most ticket sales platforms.

Fans looking to purchase tickets can do so on Ticketmaster, StubHub, Vividseats, and Viagogo.
