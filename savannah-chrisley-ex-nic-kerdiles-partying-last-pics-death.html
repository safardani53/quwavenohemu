SELF-REFLECTION

Earlier this month, Nic shared an eerie self-reflection about enjoying your time while you can.

The former pro hockey player shared a series of family photos this time, along with a message of appreciation.

"Time in this life goes by quicker and quicker each day. I will never again take the these days with them for granted and my advice is the same for everyone else.

"Love is unconditional, and it’s rare to find in this world. I’m so thankful that I have parents, siblings, nieces and nephews that love me back the way they do.

"I’ve already booked my next trip back home and I cannot wait to see them again soon! Love you guys all so much!"

SAD LOSS

Nic was tragically killed in a motorcycle accident on the morning of Saturday, September 23, 2023, in Nashville, Tennessee.

Nashville police told TMZ that the accident occurred around 3:30 in the morning, after Nic ran through a stop sign in a residential area north of central Nashville.

Authorities said Nic and his Indian motorcycle hit the driver's side of a BMW.

According to the outlet, the BMW driver immediately stopped, and Nic died from his injuries after being transported to a local hospital.

Police told TMZ that neither driver showed signs of impairment.

NIC AND SAVANNAH

Their engagement was chronicled on the Chrisley Knows Best spin-off series, Growing Up Chrisley.

The former hockey player and the reality TV star got engaged in December 2018.

The pair ended their engagement in September 2020.
